# What should I do if the Xiaomi 14 gets water-damaged?
The Xiaomi 14 adopts an IP68 splash, water, and dust-resistant ranking. The first digit indicates the device's protection against solids. The second digit indicates the device's protection against liquids. This IP ranking ensures the device remains functional and reliable in daily scenarios.**If the Xiaomi 14 gets water-damaged, please refer to the following details:** 1. Turn off the phone immediately to avoid short circuits;2. Do not charge the phone or press any buttons, as this will cause internal damage;3. Wipe the phone gently with a clean, dry cloth to remove excess water;4. Place the phone in a well-ventilated, dry area. You can also put it in a container with silica gel for 24-48 hours to absorb moisture;5. If the phone still doesn’t function properly, please contact local after-sales service center for further assistance.Please avoid using a hair dryer or heat source, as excessive heat can damage internal components.
