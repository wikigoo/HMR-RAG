# The battery of the REDMI Note 13 drains fast
**If your REDMI Note 13 is experiencing rapid battery drain and there hasn't been a significant temperature drop, please follow these steps to address the issue:** 1. Ensure your phone’s system is updated to the latest version via the Settings APP, as updates often enhance battery performance;2. Review the power consumption ranking of apps and hardware by navigating to**[Settings] >> [Battery] >>** check the ranking;3. Clear the cache that consumes more processing efficiency and power APPs by pressing the APP icon and selecting [Clear data];4. Turn off Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS, and background data for apps when they're not in use by going to**[Settings] >> [More connectivity options] >> [Reset Wi-Fi, mobile networks, and Bluetooth]**;5. Close all apps and monitor how long the battery drains from 100% to the charging prompt. A duration of a day and a half is considered normal;6. If possible, activate Airplane mode overnight. The standby mode normally consumes about 10% of power per night, compared to only about 2% in Airplane mode. If power consumption is regular in Airplane mode, your battery is likely fine;7. If the problem persists after these steps, consider performing a factory reset (**[Settings] >> [About phone] >> [Factory reset]**). Please back up your data before doing this;If the issue continues after trying these solutions, it may be a hardware problem. Please contact your local after-sales service center for further assistance.
