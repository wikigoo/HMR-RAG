# How to perform Factory reset on the REDMI 13C?
The factory reset is a process that restores the REDMI 13C to its original factory settings. This means that all data, settings, applications, and personal information stored on your phone will be erased.**To perform Factory reset on the REDMI 13C, please refer to the following steps:**1. Go to  on your phone;3. Tap **[Erase all data]** Selector not as your preference and perform1. Performing a factory reset will erase all data on your phone.
