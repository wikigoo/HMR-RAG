# Why are the photos taken by the Xiaomi 15 front camera blurry or have abnormal colors?
Thank you for your support of Xiaomi products. The camera undergoes strict professional inspection before leaving the factory to ensure it meets industry standards. In some special environments, such as low lighting, high contrast situations, or extreme conditions, normal optical phenomena can sometimes affect the clarity or color accuracy of the photos. This includes things like slight blurring, color shifts, or lens reflections, which are typical of the physics of light and optics. If these issues are affecting your usage, it is recommended to visit an authorized Xiaomi service center for inspection and assistance.
