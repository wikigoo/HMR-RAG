## What is your Xiaomi smartphone's IMEI?
### What is your Xiaomi smartphone's IMEI?
The IMEI (International Mobile Equipment Identity) is a 15-17-digit code that is given to your Xiaomi smartphone. This number is used by service providers to uniquely identify your Xiaomi handset.
### Where can you find your Xiaomi smartphone IMEI?
You can find the Xiaomi smartphone IMEI in several places, including in Settings, on the physical device, and on the original packaging.
When you participate in the Xiaomi IMEI campaign, you will need to find your IMEI number to identify your device.
#### Find your Xiaomi smartphone IMEI in Settings
Xiaomi smartphone > Go to Settings > About phone > Detailed info and specs (scroll down) > Status.
#### Find your Xiaomi Smartphone IMEI on the physical device
Type *#06# into the Phone app on your Xiaomi smartphone to find your IMEI code.
#### Find your Xiaomi smartphone IMEI on the original packaging
Look on the bottom of your Xiaomi smartphone's packaging.
### What you can do with your Xiaomi smartphone IMEI
Verify your Xiaomi smartphone
Login to the (official Xiaomi website) IMEI page and verify your Xiaomi smartphone by filling in the requested information.
Participate in the Xiaomi campaign and redeem  (official Xiaomi website) vouchers (value may differ between regions).
  * Purchase a Xiaomi product.
  * Once you have the product, go to the **IMEI web page (official Xiaomi website)**.
  * Enter the required information (Xiaomi smartphone IMEI number and email address, then enter the code sent to you by email).
