# The Xiaomi smartphone overheats while charging
**To resolve this issue, please refer to the following steps:** 1. Many Xiaomi phones support fast charging, which generates more heat compared to standard charging. The higher the charging speed, the more heat is produced. It is recommended to use a standard charger;2. Excessive ambient temperature can lead to overheating. You can charge your phone in a cool environment to help manage the temperature;3. Heavy usage, like gaming, while charging can cause overheating. It's best to close unnecessary APPs or stop high-performance activities while charging;4. Using low-quality or incompatible charging accessories can cause overheating. Please always use the original charger and cable that came with your device;5. Charging with a protective case on can trap heat and cause the phone to overheat. Remove the case during charging to ensure proper heat dissipation.
