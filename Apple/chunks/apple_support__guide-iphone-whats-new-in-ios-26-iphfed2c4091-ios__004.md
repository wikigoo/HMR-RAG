**Games.** A new app where you can keep track of all the games you’re playing, discover new ones, receive tailored recommendations, and start challenges with friends. See [Get started with the Apple Games app on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/get-started-with-the-apple-games-app-iph3aed54d2e/26/ios/26).

![The Apple Games app showing the Home tab, which displays a special event, games to continue playing, and new releases.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/158c11dce5b010c741943e50bf3ccc72.png)

**Audio recording.** Capture high-quality audio and video during video conference calls and with Camera. A new input chooser makes it easy for creators to pick the right microphone for each app. See [Record audio and video on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/record-audio-and-video-iph8dc8a5c3c/26/ios/26).

![The controls for choosing an audio input source for a FaceTime call.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/41fed1b05de4675194334c3ce27d487e.png)

**Music.** AutoMix seamlessly mixes one song to the next, just like a DJ. See [Adjust the sound quality in Music on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/adjust-the-sound-quality-iph5643d2c85/26/ios/26).

**Reminders.** (iOS 26.2) Mark a reminder as urgent to set an alarm that alerts you when it’s due. See [Add details in Reminders on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-details-iphec7a1de82/26/ios/26).

**Wallet.** Boarding passes that you save in Wallet provide access to relevant information about your flight and airport, as well as airline app features. See [Use passes in Wallet on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-passes-loyalty-cards-tickets-and-more-iphe7aa3336/26/ios/26).

![A boarding pass in the Wallet app, showing flight information and the QR code at the bottom.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/92d772c887abc0d2bf8ba26d722cab7d.png)

**Digital ID.** (iOS 26.1) In Wallet, create a Digital ID—a passport-verified ID that you can present at select TSA checkpoints in airports for domestic travel, or in supported apps or websites that require age verification.
