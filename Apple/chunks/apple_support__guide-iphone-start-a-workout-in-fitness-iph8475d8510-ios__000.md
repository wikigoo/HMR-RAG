# Start a workout in Fitness on iPhone

You can start individual workout sessions—from cardio-focused options such as High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), Outdoor Run, and Outdoor Cycle, to strength-based workouts like Functional Strength Training, Core Training, and Kickboxing. You can also set media to automatically play when you begin a workout type: If you subscribe to Apple Music, Apple Music selects media based on the workout type and the music you love. Or you can choose media yourself.

If you have just an iPhone, you can do a workout with the following workout types:

- Outdoor Walk

- Outdoor Run

- Hiking

- Outdoor Push Walking Pace

- Outdoor Push Running Pace

- Outdoor Cycle

When you connect an Apple Watch, AirPods Pro 3, Powerbeats Pro 2, or a compatible Bluetooth enabled third-party heart rate monitor, you can do a workout with workout metrics displayed on the screen.

_Note:_ If you have iPhone connected to AirPods Pro 3 or a third-party heart rate monitor, you have access to more workout types like Kickboxing and Pilates. If you have an Apple Watch, you have access to all workout types.
