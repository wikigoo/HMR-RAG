## Share your medication list

1. Go to the Health app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9778c09654aad90365a961606974a385.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do one of the following:

   - _Share your list (or just specific medications) with a loved one:_ See [Share your data in Health](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/share-your-health-data-iph5ede58c3d/26/ios/26).

     **Tip:** If you add a new medication after you start sharing, add it to your shared list.

   - _Share your list as a PDF:_ Tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png) at the bottom right, tap Medications, scroll down, tap Export PDF, tap ![the Share button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/748a151e2276ad5df4d03ec8d4506bf7.png), then choose an option.

**See also** [Learn more about your medications in Health on iPhone (U.S. only)](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/learn-more-about-your-medications-iph2fcefa8d6/26/ios/26) [View your data in Health on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-your-health-data-iphe3d379c32/26/ios/26)
