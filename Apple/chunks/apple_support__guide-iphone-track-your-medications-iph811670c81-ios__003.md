## Change the schedule for a medication or update other information

![The Medications screen in Health showing options to change the schedule for a medication.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a34aaeb1639e3562d75e3d255b611a7c.png)

1. Go to the Health app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9778c09654aad90365a961606974a385.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png) at the bottom right, then tap Medications.

3. Scroll down to Your Medications, then do any of the following:

   - _Change the schedule for a medication:_ Tap a medication, scroll down to Schedule, tap Edit, then tap a field to change it.

   - _Change the icon, nickname, or notes for a medication:_ Tap a medication, scroll down to Details, tap Edit to change the icon or tap either of the text fields, then make your changes.

   - _Reorder your list:_ Tap Edit above the list of your medications, touch and hold ![the Reorder button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/705a0755aace2f1e01e2a1ffe986c224.png) for a medication, drag it to a new position, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

   - _Archive a medication:_ Swipe left on its name in the list of your medications, then tap Archive.

   - _Make an archived medication reappear in your list of medications:_ Tap Edit above the list of your medications, then tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7c24a87974c9f3d927f73dd5edc48c38.png) next to the medication name, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png). Any schedule you previously set is not preserved.

   - _Delete a medication:_ Tap a medication, scroll down, then tap Delete Medication.

   - _Change how you receive notifications about taking medications:_ See [View and respond to notifications](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-and-respond-to-notifications-iph6534c01bc/26/ios/26) and [Change notification settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/change-notification-settings-iph7c3d96bab/26/ios/26).
