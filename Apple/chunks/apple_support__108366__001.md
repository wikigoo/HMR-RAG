## Automatically back up with iCloud Backup

1. Open the Settings app, then tap your name. Tap iCloud, then tap iCloud Backup and make sure that Back Up This \[Device\] is on.

2. Connect your device to a power source.

3. [Connect your device to a Wi-Fi network](https://support.apple.com/en-us/111107).

4. Make sure that your device's screen is locked.

5. If you get an alert that you don't have enough iCloud storage space to complete the backup, follow the onscreen steps to [buy more iCloud storage](https://support.apple.com/en-us/108349).
