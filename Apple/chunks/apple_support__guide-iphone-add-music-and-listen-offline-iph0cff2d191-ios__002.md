## Download music from Apple Music to your iPhone

Before you can download music from Apple Music to your library, you need to turn on Sync Library. Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music, then turn on Sync Library. Then follow these steps to download music.

1. Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following:

   - _Download a song, album, or playlist:_ Touch and hold music you added to your library, then tap Download.

     After you add a playlist or album, you can also tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ed5a4cfb7484e0e332ba1969a6f9a70e.png) at the top of the screen.

   - _Always download music:_ Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music, then turn on Automatic Downloads.

     Songs you add are automatically downloaded to iPhone.

   - _See download progress:_ On the Library tab, tap Downloading.

_Note:_ If the music you’re downloading is available in Dolby Atmos, the Dolby icon ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2294baa58e494523831361e0a8cac116.png) appears next to the item, and you can download it either in Dolby Atmos or in stereo. To download music in Dolby Atmos when available, go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music, then turn on Download in Dolby Atmos. See [Play music in Spatial Audio with Dolby Atmos on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/play-spatial-audio-iphac459a29e/26/ios/26).

## Delete music from your library

1. Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone.

2. Touch and hold the song, album, playlist, or music video, then tap Delete from Library.
