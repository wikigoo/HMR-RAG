# Use Smart Lists in Reminders on iPhone

In the Reminders app, you can easily filter items across all your lists using Smart Lists. You can create custom Smart Lists to automatically include items filtered by tags, dates, times, locations, flags, and priority. You can choose more than one tag (such as #gardening and #errands) and combine them with other filters.

![The Flagged Smart List in the Reminders app, which automatically collects all reminders marked with a flag into a single, consolidated list.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0c74e92cdefa0bb4680fab0513ca1dff.png)

_Note:_ All Reminders features described in this guide are available when you use [updated reminders in iCloud](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-or-remove-accounts-iph8739025dd/26/ios/26#iph8bbc4e260). Some features aren’t available when using other accounts.

## Automatically organize items using the default Smart Lists

1. Go to the Reminders app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ec6119fc5bf93b83f78120434ec8118d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Edit Lists.

3. Turn on or off the following Smart Lists:

   - _Today:_ Items scheduled for today and overdue items

   - _Scheduled:_ Items scheduled by date or time

   - _All:_ All items across all your lists

   - _Flagged:_ Items with a flag

   - _Completed:_ Items with a checkmark

   - _Assigned to Me:_ Items assigned to you in shared lists

   - _Groceries:_ Grocery items

   - _Siri Suggestions:_ Suggested items detected in Mail and Messages
