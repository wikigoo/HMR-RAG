## Set up a Focus

1. Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/88b2400e45bcf521514b7252cbb2d959.png) \> Focus, then tap a Focus—for example, Do Not Disturb, Personal, or Work.

For your Focus, you can set up the options described in the steps below, but you don’t have to set up all of them.
![A screen showing five provided Focus options—Do Not Disturb, No messages during calls, Sleep, Personal, and Work. The Share Across Devices option is on, which allows the same Focus settings to be used across your Apple devices.](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/e966b88c21db42304c7d61758d3e3330.png)
2. Specify which people and apps to allow notifications from during your Focus:

   - _People:_ Tap People, then tap Allow Notifications From. Tap ![the Add People button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/27bb9655dccf819f6b80069b98f42598.png), select contacts, tap Done, then tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/672f0597b3bef6ad7262a337e288ceb4.png) at the top of the screen. You can also silence specific people by tapping Silence Notification From instead.

   - _Apps:_ Tap Apps, then tap Allow Notifications From. Tap ![the Add Apps button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/27bb9655dccf819f6b80069b98f42598.png), select apps, tap Done, then tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/672f0597b3bef6ad7262a337e288ceb4.png) at the top of the screen. You can also silence specific apps by tapping Silence Notifications From instead.
3. Tap Options, then select any of the following:

   - _Show silenced notifications in Notification Center instead of on the Lock Screen:_ Turn off Show On Lock Screen.

   - _Hide notification badges on your silenced apps to prevent the distraction:_ Turn on Hide Notification Badges.

   - _Silence calls and notifications:_ Tap Silence Notifications, then choose Always or While Locked.

   - _Dim the Lock Screen during this Focus:_ Turn on Dim Lock Screen.
