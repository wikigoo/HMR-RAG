## Remove or reset a Photographic Style

You can remove your Photographic Style or set a new one.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Camera, tap Photographic Styles, then select “Use Standard” or choose a different style.
