## Select a Photographic Style

Select a Photographic Style to change the overall mood before you take a photo. You can also fine-tune its tone, color, and intensity.

1. Open Camera ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/71ce3044f246f5f1f828b819f1c395b6.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Camera Controls button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8ed35ca4bcbec2cdd97804e0495a881b.png) or swipe up from the camera modes at the bottom of the screen.

3. Tap Styles, then swipe left or right to preview the available styles.

4. To customize the style, do any of the following (depending on your iPhone model):

   - Drag your finger on ![Camera Control Status](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/55392a1c35be33a75747e4568b0c831b.png) to simultaneously adjust the tone and color.

   - Drag the slider to control how strongly the style affects your photos.

   - Drag the Tone and Warmth sliders to control how strongly the style affects your photos.

   - Tap ![the Reset button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b286b5723904022212d1b07955ddfbc1.png) to return to default values.
5. Tap ![the Dismiss Styles Control button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6b95c3060c7ea35acdffad3d68c54277.png) to close the Style menu.

To reset or change the style you selected, follow the instructions above, then select Standard, or choose a different style.

After you take a photo, you can further refine the Photographic Style values in the Photos app. See [Edit the Photographic Style of a photo](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/edit-photos-and-videos-iphb08064d57/26/ios/26#iphc0cdae33a).
