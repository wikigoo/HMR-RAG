## Take a portrait

![The Camera screen in Portrait mode; in the viewfinder, the subject is sharp and the background is blurred. The dial to select Portrait Lighting is open in the bottom of the frame and Studio Light is selected.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/df76d3049b2fdb62131de066b2bb1a5e.png)

1. Open Camera ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/71ce3044f246f5f1f828b819f1c395b6.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following:

   - Swipe left to select Portrait mode.

   - Tap ![the Depth button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/25cdab1e14eff2b794ff28c2bccd2ba7.png) (if a face is detected in the camera frame; on [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphde3d86c94/26/ios/26)).

   - Use the Camera Control to select Portrait mode; see [Select and adjust the Camera Control settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-the-camera-control-iph0c397b154/26/ios/26#iphf10d97496).
3. Drag ![the Portrait Lighting control](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3d400598bc5159bfe3fc68d86d0a0f2e.png) to choose a lighting effect:

   - _Natural Light:_ Sharp focus with a blurred background

   - _Studio Light:_ Bright, clean lighting

   - _Contour Light:_ Dramatic shadows with highlights and lowlights

   - _Stage Light:_ Spotlight effect against a black background

   - _Stage Light Mono:_ Stage Light effect in black and white

   - _High-Key Light Mono:_ Grayscale subject on a white background
4. Tap the Shutter button or click the Camera Control on ( [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph345af6a5d/26/ios/26)) to take the portrait.

After you take the portrait, you can remove the effect if you want to see the image without it. In the Photos app, open the photo, tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2168728be5f88df306e409efc69488a0.png), then tap Portrait to turn the effect on or off.

Your iPhone captures depth information when ![the Depth button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/25cdab1e14eff2b794ff28c2bccd2ba7.png) appears while taking photos in Photo mode, so you can apply the portrait effect later even if you didn’t use it when taking the original photo.
