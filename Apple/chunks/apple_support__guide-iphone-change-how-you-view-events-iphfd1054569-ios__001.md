## View events across multiple days

1. Go to the Calendar app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1b944caed2601c11a4cfa936cfd46342.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following:

   - _View multiple days:_ In Day view, tap the Day view icon and choose Multi Day ![the Multi Day button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6fe064faa7799f918dd3200e5f7c7b80.png).

   - _View a weekly calendar:_ In Month or Day view, rotate iPhone sideways.

## View events in a list

1. Go to the Calendar app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1b944caed2601c11a4cfa936cfd46342.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following:

   - _View a list of events for each day:_ In Month view, tap the Month view icon and choose List ![the List button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/799a4d3e34da7ceaf1bb74e6395e0700.png). Tap each day to see a list of events.

   - _View a complete list of upcoming events:_ In Day view, tap the Day view icon and choose List ![the List button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/cc6667e65b317127653dd19573c8cd59.png).
