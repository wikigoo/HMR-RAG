## Edit the video playback speed

You can slow down videos that were captured at a high frame rate for a dramatic effect.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open a video, tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2168728be5f88df306e409efc69488a0.png), then tap Video.

3. Tap ![the Playback Speed button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/582c79b06ab384d1f07b1be35236eeb8.png), then tap a playback speed.

_Note:_ The playback options available depend on the format of the video you captured. The larger the format you capture, the more options are available.

## Edit the slow-motion section of a video shot in Slo-mo mode

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open a video shot in Slo-mo mode, then tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2168728be5f88df306e409efc69488a0.png).

3. Drag the black vertical bars beneath the frame viewer to set where the video is played in slow motion.
