## Learn more about health and fitness

1. Go to the Health app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9778c09654aad90365a961606974a385.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Summary, then scroll down to Get More From Health or Articles.

3. Tap an item to learn more.

When you view the details in many health categories, recommended apps are shown in addition to your data.
