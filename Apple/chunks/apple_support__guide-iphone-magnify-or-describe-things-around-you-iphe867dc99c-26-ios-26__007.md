[Source: apple_support] [Title: Magnify or describe things around you with Magnifier on iPhone - Apple Support]

## Listen to your captured text

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png) to capture the frame, then tap ![the Reader button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/66c227b33be6046ef0d5bae03157187b.png).

Magnifier alerts you if no text is detected in the frame you captured.

3. Do any of the following:

   - _Listen to the text:_ Tap ![the Play button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9fc2474e8f975c92e61abda72cf78910.png).

   - _Pause the text:_ Tap ![the Pause button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a5f086eae69a48bd1373d00365edeef5.png).

   - _Skip backward or forward:_ Tap ![the Rewind button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9530a528673e0d50898c9ff1d9460c41.png) or ![the Forward button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e2ca2b5b7a76fb497f5ca74cc4fc2611.png).

   - _Increase or decrease the speed:_ Tap ![the Speaking Rate One Times button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/74f8bb5dd4579fbe396327e9f3c2478c.png), then select a speed.

   - _Go to a specific time:_ Drag the slider left or right.

     _Note:_ You can hide or show audio controls: Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38f95724ed0a62ccb8a071d3df51b3b2.png), then tap ![the Hide Audio Controls button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/24b79b52ef0508fdefc6154bd64e4384.png).
