## Set the specificity of your IP address location

Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> \[ _your name_\] \> iCloud > Private Relay > IP Address Location, then choose one of the following:

- Maintain General Location (for example, to see local content in Safari)

- Use Country and Time Zone (to make your location more obscure)

**See also** [Use Hide My Email in Safari on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-hide-my-email-iphbb888fde2/26/ios/26) [Apple Support article: About iCloud Private Relay](https://support.apple.com/102602)
