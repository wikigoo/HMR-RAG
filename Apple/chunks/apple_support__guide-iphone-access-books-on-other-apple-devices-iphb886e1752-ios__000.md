# Access books on other Apple devices in the Books app on iPhone

You can access your books and audiobooks in the Books app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f8320af1656515771e747cbc0eed5bd3.png) on your other Apple devices such as your iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro. You can also sync your reading position, highlights, notes, library collections, and more.

_Note:_ Apple Books isn’t available in all countries or regions. See the Apple Support article [Availability of Apple Media Services](https://support.apple.com/118205).

## Access your books on your other devices

To keep your Books content and information updated across your other Apple devices, sign in to the same [Apple Account](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphfd5021a18/26/ios/26) on each device, then do any of the following:

- _Automatically download purchases made on other devices:_ Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Books, then turn on Purchases from Other Devices.

- _Sync reading position, bookmarks, notes, and highlights:_ Go to Settings > \[ _your name_\] \> iCloud, tap See All next to Saved to iCloud, then turn on Books.

- _Sync Home, Library, and collections:_ Go to Settings > Apps > Books, then below Syncing, turn on Home and iCloud Drive.
