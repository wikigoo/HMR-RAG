## View categories and home status

The Lights, Climate, Security, Speakers and TVs, and Water categories let you quickly access all the relevant accessories organized by room.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap the Home tab.

Below your home’s name, buttons show the status of accessories belonging to a category—for example, a Lights category that shows “3 on.”

3. Tap one of the category buttons to show all accessories within that category, organized by room.

4. While viewing a category, tap the icon on the left side of an accessory tile to perform an action—turn a light or group of lights on or off, start playing music on a HomePod, or lock the front door.

## View Activity History

When you have a home hub using the [new version of Apple Home](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/upgrade-to-the-new-version-of-apple-home-iphaeb0ce2d3/26/ios/26) and latest software, all the members of your home (except guests) can view the activity history for accessories like garage doors, locks, security systems, and [adaptive temperature](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/adaptive-temperature-clean-energy-guidance-iphdf6fde3d8/26/ios/26#iph8828aa73f) thermostats. For example, you can learn when a door was unlocked and who unlocked it.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. On the Home tab, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de35ce63d633769b9bfeb184c874452.png), then tap Home Settings.

3. Go to Activity, then tap One Month to view up to 30 days of activity.

4. In the Home tab, tap the Security or Climate category, then tap Home Activity to show the Activity History.
