## Keep your Focus settings up to date across all your Apple devices

You can use the same Focus settings on all your Apple devices where you’re [signed in to the same Apple Account](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph39beaede9/18.0/ios/18.0).

1. Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/88b2400e45bcf521514b7252cbb2d959.png) \> Focus.

2. Turn on Share Across Devices.

_Note:_ When you turn on Share Across Devices and turn on Share Focus Status, your Focus can show that you have notifications silenced. For example, if you silence notifications when you [Set up a Focus](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/set-up-a-focus-iphd6288a67f/18.0/ios/18.0#ipheb6ee12a0), people who text you will see that your notifications are silenced. You can change this setting for specific people on their contact card.
