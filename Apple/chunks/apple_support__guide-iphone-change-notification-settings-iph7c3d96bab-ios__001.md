## Prevent notification previews from appearing on the Lock Screen

You can choose when to have notification previews from an app appear on your Lock Screen.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Notifications, then tap an app below Notification Style.

3. Scroll down, tap Show Previews, then select one of the following:

   - Always

   - When Unlocked

   - Never

## Change the frequency of notifications

To minimize interruptions from notifications throughout the day, you can schedule a notification summary that includes notifications for several apps at once.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Notifications, then tap Scheduled Summary.

3. Turn on Scheduled Summary, then tap Continue (if it appears).

4. Schedule times for the notification summary.

5. Tap A to Z, turn on the apps to include in your notification summary, then tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/672f0597b3bef6ad7262a337e288ceb4.png) at the top of the screen.

6. To choose when you want notification previews to appear, tap Show Previews, select an option, then tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/672f0597b3bef6ad7262a337e288ceb4.png) at the top of the screen.

Previews can include things like text (from Messages and Mail) and invitation details (from Calendar). You can override this setting for individual apps.

When you use Focus, it delays the delivery of notifications on iPhone to prevent interruptions. You can schedule a time to receive a summary of the notifications you missed. See [Schedule a notification summary](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-and-respond-to-notifications-iph6534c01bc/26/ios/26#iphb9de540c3).
