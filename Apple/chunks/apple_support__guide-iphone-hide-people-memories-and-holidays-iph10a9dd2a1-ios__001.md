## Turn off memories, holidays, and other featured content

You can turn off memories or featured photos in the Photos app, and prevent holiday events in your home country or region from appearing in memories and featured content.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Tap your account in the top-right corner.

4. Do any of the following:

   - _Hide featured content and memories:_ Turn off Show Featured Content to prevent Photos from automatically generating featured photos and memories.

   - _Turn off holiday events:_ Turn off Show Holiday Events to prevent Photos from creating memories and featured content about holidays.
