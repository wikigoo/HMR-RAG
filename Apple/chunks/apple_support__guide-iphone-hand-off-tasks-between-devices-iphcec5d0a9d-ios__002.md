## Hand off a task from your Apple Watch

If you have an Apple Watch, you can hand off tasks from certain apps on your Apple Watch to your iPhone. To learn more, see [Handoff tasks from Apple Watch](https://support.apple.com/guide/watch/apdc40081790).

## If Handoff isn’t working

- Make sure that both devices have [Wi-Fi](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph5f955a936/26/ios/26) and [Bluetooth](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph453f556d6/26/ios/26) turned on, and you’re signed in to the same [Apple Account](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphfd5021a18/26/ios/26).

- Check that your app supports Handoff. Calendar, Contacts, FaceTime, Keynote, Mail, Maps, Pages, Numbers, Reminders, and Safari, as well as many third-party apps, allow Handoff.

**Tip:** When Handoff is on, you can use [Universal Clipboard](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/copy-and-paste-between-devices-iph220ea8dca/26/ios/26#iph9e17640f4) to copy and paste text, images, photos, and videos across devices.
