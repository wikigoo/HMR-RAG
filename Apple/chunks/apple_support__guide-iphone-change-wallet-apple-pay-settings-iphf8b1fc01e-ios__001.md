## Customize your Wallet & Apple Pay settings

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Wallet & Apple Pay, then set any of the following:

   - _Location:_ Control the [location information you share](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/control-the-location-information-you-share-iph3dd5f9be/26/ios/26).

   - _Siri & Search:_ Allow [Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-siri-suggestions-on-or-off-iph6f94af287/26/ios/26) to make suggestions across apps based on the way you use Wallet & Apple Pay. You can also allow suggestions and content from Wallet & Apple Pay to appear in [Search](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/search-on-iphone-iph3c511548/26/ios/26) and [widgets](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-edit-and-remove-widgets-iphb8f1bf206/26/ios/26).

   - _Notifications:_ Change [notification settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/change-notification-settings-iph7c3d96bab/26/ios/26).

   - _Live Activities:_ Turn on or off [Live Activities on the Lock Screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/access-features-from-the-lock-screen-iphcd5c65ccf/26/ios/26#iph3f3ff5648).

   - _Cellular Data:_ Turn on or off [cellular data usage](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-or-change-cellular-data-settings-iph3dd5f213/26/ios/26#iph66b77f44b).

   - _Apple Pay Defaults:_ [Change your default card, address, and preferences for Apple Pay](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/change-wallet-apple-pay-settings-iphf8b1fc01e/26/ios/26#iph7d4dbdf50).

   - _Apple Cash:_ Turn on or off [Apple Cash](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-apple-cash-iph385cf0980/26/ios/26#iph024874f75).

   - _AutoFill Cards:_ [Fill in payment information](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/fill-in-payment-information-iphf101498e9/26/ios/26).

   - _Express Transit Card:_ Choose cards to pay for transit automatically without requiring authentication. See [Pay for your ride with Express Transit](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/pay-for-transit-iph3fedf8b29/26/ios/26#iphb09100aa1).

   - _Hide Expired Passes:_ Turn on to [archive old passes](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/remove-cards-and-passes-iph3dd32dffe/26/ios/26#iph0f397db88).

   - _Order tracking:_ Purchases from participating merchants are automatically added to Wallet for [order tracking](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/us
