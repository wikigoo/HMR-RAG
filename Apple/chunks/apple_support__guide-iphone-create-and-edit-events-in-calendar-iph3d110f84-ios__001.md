## Add an event

1. Go to the Calendar app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1b944caed2601c11a4cfa936cfd46342.png) on your iPhone.

2. In Day view, tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0d07cb7185e2d87af8a9ad8b4ace6995.png) at the top of the screen.

3. Enter the title of the event.

4. Tap Location or Video Call, then enter a physical location or tap FaceTime to enter a video link for a remote event.

You can also copy a FaceTime link you created or received and paste it in the Location field. See [Create a link to a FaceTime call](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-a-facetime-link-iphfc9b58aaa/26/ios/26).

5. Enter the start and end times for the event, the travel time, invitees, attachments, and so on. (Swipe up, if necessary, to enter all the meeting information.)

6. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ba2d818fa1343c4620a862e45c5ba4c2.png).

## Add an alert

You can set an alert to be reminded of an event beforehand.

1. Go to the Calendar app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1b944caed2601c11a4cfa936cfd46342.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap the event, then tap Edit.

3. In the event details, tap Alert.

4. Choose when you want to be reminded.

For example, “At time of event,” “5 minutes before,” or another choice.

_Note:_ If you add the address of the event’s location, Calendar uses Apple Maps to look up locations, traffic conditions, and transit options to tell you when it’s time to leave.

5. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/49fec30705abe44e61a4a36f9658a0a2.png).
