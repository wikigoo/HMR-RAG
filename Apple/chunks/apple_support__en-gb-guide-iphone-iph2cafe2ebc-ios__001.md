## Turn HDR video off and on

On [supported models](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/aside/iphc1127fd5d/26/ios/26), iPhone records video in Dolby Vision HDR for true-to-life color and contrast. To turn off HDR video recording, go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Camera > Record Video, then turn off HDR Video or HDR (depending on your model).
