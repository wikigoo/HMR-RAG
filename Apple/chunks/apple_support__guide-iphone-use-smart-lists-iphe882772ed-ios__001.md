## Make a custom Smart List

1. Go to the Reminders app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ec6119fc5bf93b83f78120434ec8118d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Add List button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/d9e8f0f8e558a6f1ee445c2e1b20e088.png).

3. Enter a name for the list, tap List Type, then choose Smart List.

4. Tap Edit Filters, choose one or more filters, then choose to include items matching any or all of the selected filters. You can filter by tags, dates, locations, and more. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

5. Choose a color and symbol for the list (optional).

6. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

## Convert a list to a Smart List

When you convert a list, its items are moved to the top level of the default list and tagged with the name of the Smart List.

_Note:_ You can’t convert a shared list.

1. Go to the Reminders app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ec6119fc5bf93b83f78120434ec8118d.png) on your iPhone.

2. View the list you want to convert.

3. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), tap Show List Info, scroll to the bottom, then tap Convert to Smart List.

**See also** [Tag items in Reminders on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/tag-items-iph474a6685c/26/ios/26) [Organize multiple lists in Reminders on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/organize-multiple-lists-iph2c6cf708e/26/ios/26)
