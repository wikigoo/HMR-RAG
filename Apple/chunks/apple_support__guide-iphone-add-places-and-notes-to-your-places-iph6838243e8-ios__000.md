# Add places and notes to your Places in Maps on iPhone

You can find pins, places, guides, and routes in one place: your Places in the Maps app. Add your own personal notes—visible only to you—to saved places, or pin your most frequently visited places for easier access.

![The Places screen in Maps, which shows a list of place categories like Pinned, Saved Places, and Guides. Below this, a Recently Added section lists viewed locations.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/01ca83d2f97141ad6335d883f85f9d31.png)

## Save a place

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. While looking at a place card, do one of the following:

   - Tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0d07cb7185e2d87af8a9ad8b4ace6995.png). It turns to ![the Added button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ae89099ef98753805310a8a593327c52.png).

   - Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Add to Places.
