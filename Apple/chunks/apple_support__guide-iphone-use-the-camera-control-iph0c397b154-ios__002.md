## Customize how you open the Camera Control

You can change the number of clicks it takes to open Camera with the Camera Control from one click to two.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Camera, tap Camera Control, then tap Single Click or Double Click.

You can also adjust accessibility options for the Camera Control, such as the pressure required to lightly press or the speed required to lightly double-press. See [Adjust accessibility settings for the Camera Control](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/adjust-camera-control-settings-iph22c8345f8/26/ios/26).

## Use visual intelligence with Camera Control

If Apple Intelligence\* is turned on, you can use visual intelligence with Camera Control to quickly learn more about the places and objects around you. For example, get details about a restaurant or business, have text translated or read aloud, find out where to buy something you see, and more. See [Use visual intelligence on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-visual-intelligence-iph12eb1545e/26/ios/26).
