## Choose audio for a workout

You can set media to automatically play when you begin a workout type—either media selected for you based on what you normally listen to, or media you choose yourself.

1. Go to the Fitness app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4a171706431000fd128f989d724dd5ca.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Workout, scroll to a workout type, then tap ![the Media button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/960aa53985e64fde7d76889d4f7e9507.png).

3. Turn on Autoplay Media.

4. If you subscribe to Apple Music, you can have media play automatically based on the workout type and the music you listen to. Tap Picked for You.

5. To choose your own media, tap Choose Media, tap Add Media, then do one of the following:

   - _Choose a suggested option:_ Tap an option below Suggestions.

     **Tip:** To view more suggestions, tap Music or Podcasts below Sources.

   - _Choose a playlist:_ Tap Music, tap Playlists, then choose a playlist.
6. When you’re finished, tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8bcd220bb69165d0a7a2c11d4a868121.png).

_Note:_ Music or other audio you’re already listening to before you begin your workout continues to play.
