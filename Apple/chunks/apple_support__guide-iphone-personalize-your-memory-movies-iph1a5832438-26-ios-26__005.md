## Edit the memory title, subtitle, and key photo

You can edit the title or subtitle of a memory and change the key photo that appears with the title of the memory.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Swipe left under Memories to scroll through a selection of your memories, or tap Memories to view them all.

4. Tap a memory to view the collection.

5. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8a1f0a8df86eb1c80021954b0f092e6e.png), tap the current title, then do any of the following:

   - _Edit the memory title or subtitle:_ Enter a new title or subtitle in the text fields.

   - _Change the key photo:_ Tap the photo icon in the bottom-right corner of the current key photo, then select a new photo in the photo grid.
6. Tap Done.
