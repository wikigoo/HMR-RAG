## Delete a recorded call

1. Go to the Notes app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/752a4ee174395508df3192592b50ef60.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap a note in the Call Recordings folder, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5319bdf46371a4f82e24e84aa7dee3a9.png), then tap Delete.

_Note:_ Deleting the audio recording also deletes the transcript.

## Turn off Call Recording

Call Recording is on by default, but you can turn it off.

1. Go to the Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) app on your iPhone.

2. Tap Apps, then tap Phone.

3. Tap Call Recording, then turn off Call Recording.
