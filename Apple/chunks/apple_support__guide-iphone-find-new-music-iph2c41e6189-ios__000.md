# Find new music with Apple Music on iPhone

When you [subscribe to Apple Music](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/subscribe-to-apple-music-iphcfadf0701/26/ios/26), you can browse new and noteworthy music, music videos, playlists, and more.
