## Set an alarm

You can set alarms for any time, including one for the time you want to wake up. Any alarm you set up in Clock is unrelated to a sleep schedule.

1. Go to the Clock app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38d6e8b6bb0cf0e0575d007c6d53fb29.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Alarms, then tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1a9bda2390113800474d880037fe3324.png).

3. Set the time, then choose any of the following options:

   - _Repeat:_ Choose the days of the week you want the alarm to repeat.

   - _Label:_ Give the alarm a name, like “Water the plants.”

   - _Sound:_ Choose a vibration, song, or ringtone.

   - _Snooze:_ Turn on to see a Snooze button with your alarm.

   - _Snooze Duration:_ Choose how long to pause the alarm.
4. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/780f7463300e41a2f875f912ab290a0c.png).

To change the alarm, tap the alarm time. Or tap Edit at the top left, then tap the alarm time.

## Change the alarm’s volume

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Sounds & Haptics.

3. Under Ringtone and Alerts, drag the volume slider.

**Tip:** To use the volume buttons on your device to change the alarm volume, turn on Change with Buttons.

Your alarm sounds even when you:

- Turn on [Silent mode](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/silence-iphone-iph81c7fd7d1/26/ios/26#iphcc98fdd04).

- Turn on Do Not Disturb (or another [Focus](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/set-up-a-focus-iphd6288a67f/26/ios/26)).

- Connect headphones to your iPhone. The alarm plays at a set volume through both the built-in speakers on your iPhone as well as your headphones.

## Turn off an alarm

1. Go to the Clock app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38d6e8b6bb0cf0e0575d007c6d53fb29.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Alarms.

3. Tap the button next to the alarm time.
