## Export a note as a Markdown file

You can export your notes as Markdown files to integrate your notes into writing, content, and other workflows that support Markdown.

1. Go to the Notes app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/454094731616c01a657acf1f45a61c55.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open the note you want to export.

3. Tap ![the Share button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f641f1d538e27d78e9d83a0d0359a02b.png), tap Export as Markup, then choose how to send or save your note.

## Print a note

1. Go to the Notes app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/454094731616c01a657acf1f45a61c55.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open the note you want to print.

3. Tap ![the Share button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f641f1d538e27d78e9d83a0d0359a02b.png), then tap Print.

4. Choose the printer and other options, then tap Print.
