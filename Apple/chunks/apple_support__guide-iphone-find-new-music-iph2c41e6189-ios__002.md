## Discover your most-played music of the year

For every year you’ve subscribed to Apple Music (and played enough music to become eligible), Apple Music creates a Replay personal chart, ranking the songs you’ve played the most.

1. Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Home, swipe down to Replay: Your Top Music, then tap an option (for example, a year).

You can also see all your insights throughout the year on your iPhone or at [replay.music.apple.com](https://replay.music.apple.com/). See the Apple Support article [How to get your Apple Music Replay](https://support.apple.com/109356).

## Discover music you’ve never played before

Using your listening history and taste profile, Apple Music recommends music you may like that you’ve never played before.

**Siri:** [Ask Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/summon_siri/26/ios/26) something like, “Play my Discovery Station.” [Learn how to use Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-on-and-activate-siri-iph83aad8922/26/ios/26).

1. Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Home, then swipe down to Stations for You.

3. Tap Discovery Station.

## Turn on or off new music notifications

1. Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the My Account button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/75c119c299421ca48b9edf9b28eebda5.png) or your profile picture.

3. Tap Notifications, then turn on (or off) New Music.
