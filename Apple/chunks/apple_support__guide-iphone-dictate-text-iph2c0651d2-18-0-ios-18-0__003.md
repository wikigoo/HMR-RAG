## Turn off Dictation

1. Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/88b2400e45bcf521514b7252cbb2d959.png) \> General > Keyboard.

2. Turn off Enable Dictation.
