## Create original images in Freeform

You can create images with Image Playground directly in the Freeform app.

1. Go to the Freeform app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8adc98e6e3712b407ec28063cfd0709b.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open a board, or tap ![the New Board button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/678b074491ca5209414815750e75fea1.png) to start a new one.

3. Do one of the following:

   - _Create a new image:_ Tap ![the Insert Media button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/025baf2be112a826d2a87dbb0e230d26.png) in the toolbar, tap Image Playground, then follow instructions to [create an image](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/create-original-images-with-image-playground-iph0063238b5/26/ios/26#iph490bc6603).

   - _Use elements from your board:_ [Select](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/draw-or-handwrite-iphe749c1957/26/ios/26#iph0d8bb647f) up to six images, drawings, text boxes, stickies, or shapes that you want to use, tap them, then tap Add to Playground.

   - _Replace an existing image with an image created in Image Playground:_ [Add an image](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/add-images-scans-links-and-other-files-iphd301a8bf1/26/ios/26), select the image, tap ![the Modify Image button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/297c1374fe7c50f0c764d49e1a413977.png), tap Replace, tap Image Playground, then follow instructions to [create an image](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/create-original-images-with-image-playground-iph0063238b5/26/ios/26#iph490bc6603).
4. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/02981b7364cd7a2928906668ec4494fc.png) to add the image to your board.

You can also use Image Playground in other apps—such as Keynote, Pages, Apple Invites, and supported third-party apps.

You can block access to image creation features, like Image Playground, in Screen Time settings. See [Block access to image creation features](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/iphone/block-access-to-apple-intelligence-features-iph3fed3f2c3/26/ios/26#iphb11516d73).
