# Track your medications in Health on iPhone

You can track and manage the medications, vitamins, and supplements you take.

**Siri:** On [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph229706d23/26/ios/26), [ask Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/summon_siri/26/ios/26) something like, “Log my 6AM medications as taken” (not available in all languages or regions). [Learn how to use Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-on-and-activate-siri-iph83aad8922/26/ios/26).

![The Medications screen in Health showing the date and a log for medications.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c5d7918ee80f53ba5ba3c1ad4ef77829.png)

_Note:_ Some features are not available in all countries or regions. The Medications feature is not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Additional information is available on the labels of your medications. Consult your healthcare provider prior to making any decisions related to your health.
