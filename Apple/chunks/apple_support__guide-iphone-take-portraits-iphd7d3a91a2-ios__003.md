## Adjust the background blur

Use the Depth Control slider to adjust the level of background blur in your portraits.

1. Open Camera ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/71ce3044f246f5f1f828b819f1c395b6.png) on your iPhone.

2. Swipe left to select [Portrait mode](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/camera-basics-iph263472f78/26/ios/26#iphac6618d52) or stay in Photo mode and tap ![the Depth Off button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/25cdab1e14eff2b794ff28c2bccd2ba7.png) on ( [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphde3d86c94/26/ios/26)).

3. Tap ![the Camera Controls button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8ed35ca4bcbec2cdd97804e0495a881b.png) or swipe up from Portrait mode at the bottom of the screen.

4. Tap Aperture, then drag the slider below the frame right or left to adjust the effect.

5. Tap ![the Camera Control Status button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6b95c3060c7ea35acdffad3d68c54277.png) to close the Depth Control slider.

On [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph345af6a5d/26/ios/26), you can also adjust the depth control using the Camera Control. See [Select and adjust the Camera Control settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-the-camera-control-iph0c397b154/26/ios/26#iphf10d97496).

After you capture a portrait, you can use the Depth Control slider in the Photos app to further adjust the background blur effect. See [Edit Portrait mode photos](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/edit-portrait-mode-photos-iph310a9a220/26/ios/26).
