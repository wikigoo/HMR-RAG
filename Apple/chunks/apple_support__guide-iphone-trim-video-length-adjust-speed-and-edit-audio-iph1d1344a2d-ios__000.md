# Trim video length, adjust speed, and edit audio on iPhone

In the Photos app, you can trim a video you recorded on your iPhone to change where it starts and stops. You can also adjust the playback speed, and change the portion of a video that appears in slow motion when you record in Slo-mo mode.

When you record a video with Spatial Audio, you can use Audio Mix to edit the sound in the Photos app (available on [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph5da84b7f8/26/ios/26)).

## Trim a video

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open a video, then tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2168728be5f88df306e409efc69488a0.png).

3. Drag either end of the frame viewer below the video to adjust the start and stop times, then tap Done.

4. Tap Save Video to save only the trimmed video, or Save Video as New Clip to save both versions of the video.
![The Edit screen in the Photos app with a video playing in the center. In the frame viewer below the video, frames to include in the trimmed video are selected and outlined in yellow. On the bottom of the screen are the Video, Audio Mix, Adjust, Filters, and Crop buttons; the Video button is selected.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/d219779de7a63de68f26df7b37890d7a.png)

To undo edits after you save, open the video, tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2168728be5f88df306e409efc69488a0.png), then tap Revert.

_Note:_ A video saved as a new clip can’t be reverted to the original.
