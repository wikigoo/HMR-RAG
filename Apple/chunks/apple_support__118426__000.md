# How to back up your iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch

If you back up your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch, you'll have a copy of your information to use in case your device is ever replaced, lost, or damaged.

## Choose a backup method

Before you get started, learn the [difference between iCloud backups and backups you make using your computer](https://support.apple.com/kb/ht204136). Then choose the method that's best for you.
