## Find text in a PDF

1. Go to the Preview app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ca7f2c2acd3368373c4d43c309574cf1.png) on your iPhone.

2. [Open a PDF](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/open-and-view-pdfs-and-images-iph7a0cdad0c/26/ios/26#iph47b7f742f) you want to search.

3. Tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png).

4. Optionally, tap ![the Search Options button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/bc9621b70009a1ef45b0454cc056116e.png) in the search field and do any of the following:

   - _Exclude matches where the search text is part of another word:_ Select Whole Word.

   - _Only include results that match the capitalization of the search text:_ Select Match Case.
5. Enter a word or phrase in the search field.

If you enter a phrase, Preview searches for that exact phrase.

6. Tap ![the Next Result button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/d9ef375b37f3e012ba5381d26fd9d6a8.png) or ![the Previous Result button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b63d64e291d5e3d92005d1e487609e13.png) to go to the next or previous search result.

7. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7c29db565673f106ec2610dbf4a90268.png) to close the search results.
