## Change the look of a memory movie

Use Memory looks to apply consistent color and style adjustments to all the photos in a memory movie.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Swipe left under Memories to scroll through a selection of your memories, or tap Memories to view them all.

4. Tap ![the Play button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de4289510e31551c9b519237047a4ca.png) in the bottom-right corner of a memory to play it.

5. Tap the screen, then tap ![the Memory Mixes button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/309496ba56eb4ffc1549edc5e4a84a32.png).

6. Tap ![the Filters button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8abec477c96730d2a869fc2928282088.png), tap a look, then tap Done.
