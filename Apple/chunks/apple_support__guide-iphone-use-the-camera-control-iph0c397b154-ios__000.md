# Use the Camera Control on iPhone

The Camera Control on ( [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph345af6a5d/26/ios/26)) lets you quickly open your iPhone camera and access common camera settings. The location of the Camera Control is shown below.

![An arrow points to the location of the Camera Control on the side of iPhone.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/fa6ad5fe06b2137eea07499439cb869c.png)

## Open Camera with the Camera Control

Click the Camera Control to open Camera ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/71ce3044f246f5f1f828b819f1c395b6.png).

## Take a photo or video with the Camera Control

1. Click the Camera Control to open Camera ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/71ce3044f246f5f1f828b819f1c395b6.png).

2. Click again to take a photo, or click and hold to record a video.

_Note:_ If your camera is set to Video mode when Camera opens, click to start recording.

## Settings available with the Camera Control

You can select and adjust any of the following settings with the Camera Control:

- _Exposure:_ Brighten or darken the scene by increasing or decreasing the exposure.

- _Depth:_ Increase or decrease the background depth-of-field effect (this setting turns on Portrait mode if it wasn’t already selected).

- _Zoom:_ Zoom in or out.

- _Cameras:_ Switch between cameras, including the front-facing camera.

- _Styles:_ Change the [Photographic Style](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-photographic-styles-iph629d2cd37/26/ios/26).

- _Tone:_ Lighten or deepen the mood by increasing or decreasing the tone.

_Note:_ Not all controls are available in all [camera modes](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/camera-basics-iph263472f78/26/ios/26#iphac6618d52).
