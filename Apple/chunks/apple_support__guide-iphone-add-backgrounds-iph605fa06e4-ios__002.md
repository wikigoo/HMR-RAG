## Revert to a plain background

_Note:_ Removing a background affects all participants in a conversation.

1. Go to the Messages app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ad1f1e874b79cd34473c266e98f9cbd0.png) on your iPhone.

2. Go to the conversation with a background you want to remove.

3. Tap the contact or group icon at the top of the conversation, then tap Backgrounds.

4. Tap None.

## Turn conversation backgrounds off

When you turn conversation backgrounds off, others in a conversation still see any background chosen for a conversation.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Apps, then tap Messages.

3. Turn Conversation Backgrounds off.
