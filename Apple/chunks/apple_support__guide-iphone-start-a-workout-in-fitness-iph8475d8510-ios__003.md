## Use the workout controls

During a workout, you can pause, mark a segment, [send a Check In](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-check-in-iphc143bb7e9/26/ios/26#iph83285f205), lock controls, and more. When you finish a workout, you can view a summary of how you did.

1. Go to the Fitness app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4a171706431000fd128f989d724dd5ca.png) on your iPhone.

2. [Start a workout](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/start-a-workout-in-fitness-iph8475d8510/26/ios/26#iph2d72baf57), then do any of the following:

   - _Pause the workout:_ Tap ![the Pause button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b6aad128109b0598370d877b45a0f41d.png).

   - _Resume the workout:_ Tap ![the Resume button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/12a02f28edef20c789378cbdd5794a86.png).

   - _Mark a segment:_ Tap ![the Segments button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/28f5451ed77382293fde5c4aaee87226.png).

   - _Connect a heart rate device:_ Tap ![the Heart Rate Devices button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7493f45b28c115223f51d17eb3433aaa.png).

   - _End the workout:_ Swipe up from the bottom of the screen, then tap End Workout. Review the workout summary, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8bcd220bb69165d0a7a2c11d4a868121.png).

   - _Mute Workout Buddy:_ Swipe up from the bottom of the screen, then tap Mute.

   - _Start a Check-In:_ Swipe up from the bottom of the screen, tap Check-In, then follow the onscreen instructions.

   - _Stop viewing metrics on iPhone (Apple Watch required):_ Swipe up from the bottom of the screen, then tap Stop Viewing.

     _Note:_ To start viewing again, swipe right on your Apple Watch, then tap View.

   - _Lock the workout controls:_ Swipe up from the bottom of the screen, then tap Lock Controls.

**See also** [See your activity summary in Fitness on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/see-your-activity-summary-iph4c34a8a95/26/ios/26) [Customize a workout in Fitness on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-a-workout-iphbcd56be45/26/ios/26)
