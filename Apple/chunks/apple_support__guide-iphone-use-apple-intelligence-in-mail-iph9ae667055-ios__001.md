## Use Smart Reply in Mail

1. Go to the Mail app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/352a64cda8fca90aa29ad6f4f6185ee5.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap an email, tap ![the Reply button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/54b80d14295606b13cd15c37e62e32de.png), then tap Reply.

3. In the keyboard, tap a suggested reply.

Apple Intelligence drafts a reply in the email. To refine your reply, see [Use Writing Tools with Apple Intelligence on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/find-the-right-words-with-writing-tools-iph6f08da1d2/26/ios/26).

4. When you’re ready to send your reply, tap ![the Send button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f5dabff8f401af1580540b6ab7aedcb8.png).

## Summarize your email

Apple Intelligence can help you summarize a complex email, or catch up on a long email thread in seconds.

1. Go to the Mail app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/352a64cda8fca90aa29ad6f4f6185ee5.png) on your iPhone.

A short summary automatically appears under each email in your inbox.

2. Tap an email, then tap Summarize.

A summary appears at the top of the screen. If the message is part of an email thread, the contents of the thread are summarized.
