**Family.** During device set up, parents can specify if they are setting up a device for an older child, age 13–17, and age-appropriate protections are automatically enabled. See [Share age ranges with apps on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/share-age-ranges-with-apps-iphe56aab53d/26/ios/26).

![The onboarding screen for Age-Appropriate Experiences in Apps features.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a9609e0ba89ee206a7f8554b37c25699.png)

**Journal.** Create multiple journals for different aspects of your life. As you write, include drawings inline with text, and then view the location of your journal entries on a map. See [Create multiple journals](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/write-in-your-journal-iph9824e83ce/26/ios/26#iph025e225ef), [Add formatting, photos, and more in Journal on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-formatting-photos-and-more-iph492ee70a8/26/ios/26), and [View and search your journal entries on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-and-search-journal-entries-iph6257be047/26/ios/26).

![The Journal app showing a journal entry. At the top of the entry is a grid which includes multiple photos and a map.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2aa68335cc45ce470339aa14b41b975d.png)

**CarPlay.** New design makes CarPlay an easier and safer way to use your iPhone while you drive. Tapbacks in Messages make it easy to quickly respond to messages you receive while driving. Widgets let you keep up with your favorite apps at a glance and Live Activities on the dashboard let you keep track of events in real time. See [React with Tapbacks](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/send-and-receive-text-messages-iphb5d45624c/26/ios/26#iphcb39496c2), [Add a widget for CarPlay](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-widgets-iphb4d6a0bbb/26/ios/26#iph252139641), and [View Live Activities in CarPlay](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-live-activities-iph3d92302ee/26/ios/26).
