You can [find the downloaded item in your library](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/manage-your-library-iph3ecbb37ad/26/ios/26) and watch it even when iPhone isn’t connected to the internet.

## Change audio options during playback

1. During playback, tap the screen to show the playback controls.

2. Tap ![the Audio Track button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ebd585f0d6bc186a983b8830cfd99f24.png), then tap Enhance Dialogue.

3. Tap one of the following:

   - _Enhance More:_ Makes dialogue very prominent in the mix.

   - _Enhance:_ Makes dialogue more prominent in the mix.

   - _Off:_ Turns Enhance Dialogue off.
