# What’s new in iOS 26

**Lock Screen.** The time dynamically adapts its position based on your Lock Screen photo or wallpaper and always keeps the subject in view. When you move your iPhone, your Lock Screen photo comes to life with a new 3D effect. See [Create a custom iPhone Lock Screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-a-custom-lock-screen-iph4d0e6c351/26/ios/26).

![The iPhone Lock Screen.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/22e149f75fab72b818e2bb626c6c986a.png)

**Home Screen.** App icons on your Home Screen have a new look that you can tint with different colors or turn light, dark, or clear. See [Customize apps and widgets on the iPhone Home Screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-apps-and-widgets-on-the-home-screen-iph385473442/26/ios/26).

![The iPhone Home Screen with tinted app icons.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/85ab08a62030702fec17ed3a4ad33295.png)

**Apple Intelligence.\*** Create custom Genmoji using existing emoji, combinations of emoji, or descriptive text. Change the look and expressions of Genmoji and Image Playground creations that are based on people from your Photos library. Design custom backgrounds for your Messages conversations. Live Translation automatically translates texts in Messages, shows live translated captions in FaceTime calls, and provides spoken translations for Phone calls. Use visual intelligence to learn more about what you’re viewing on your iPhone screen. See [Create original images with Image Playground on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-original-images-with-image-playground-iph0063238b5/26/ios/26), [Create your own emoji with Genmoji on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-your-own-emoji-with-genmoji-iph4e76f5667/26/ios/26), [Create and edit original backgrounds with Image Playground](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-apple-intelligence-in-messages-iph64709c5c3/26/ios/26#iph4452ecda1), [Translate messages, calls, and conversations on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/translate-messages-calls-and-conversations-iph22b72984d/26/ios/26), and [Learn more about the content on your iPhone screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-visual-intelligence-iph12eb1545e/26/ios/26#iph4d370546c).
