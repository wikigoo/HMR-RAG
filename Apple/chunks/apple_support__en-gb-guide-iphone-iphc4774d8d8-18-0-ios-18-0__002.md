## Watch, listen, and play together

To get started sharing music or video together, or working out together during a FaceTime call, tap ![the Share Content button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/e6784006b85b19a0d11885ba22958cc1.png) in the FaceTime controls (if you don’t see ![the Share Content button](https://help.apple.com/assets/683A54F5227CBBDB98051B96/683A54FC545676DF600F97D4/en_US/e6784006b85b19a0d11885ba22958cc1.png), tap the screen).

Scroll through the apps below Listen and Play Together, then select one (for example, Apple TV app, Music, or Fitness).
