## Download content

![The Apple TV tab showing a list of episodes of an Apple Original. Buttons to download the entire season and each episode appear on the right side of the screen.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2d689bf9343c1943d9fd1175176a58f5.png)[Stream _Hijack_ on the Apple TV app with a subscription](https://tv.apple.com/us/show/hijack/umc.cmc.1dg08zn0g3zx52hs8npoj5qe3)

In the Apple TV app, you can download movies and episodes of a TV show. You can also download a full season of a TV show.

1. Go to the Apple TV app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/abed9ba23a400d9fcb746e6d2e1c3a59.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap an item to see its details.

3. Do any of the following (not all options are available for all titles):

   - _Download movies:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner.

   - _Download an episode of a TV show:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner, tap a season, then tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a2bc1b532bdbca3dd86ef6d8380c53e0.png) next to the episode you want to download.

   - _Download a full season of a TV show:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner, tap a season, then tap the season button above the list of episodes.

You can [find the downloaded item in your library](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/manage-your-library-iph3ecbb37ad/26/ios/26) and watch it even when iPhone isn’t connected to the internet.
