# Change how you view events in Calendar on iPhone

You can view events across the span of a month, week, or multiple days, or view a list of upcoming events.

![A calendar in Month view showing events and reminders.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7a167465221d4c1893db79e08a8dbb49.png)

## See events in Month view

You can change your Month view to show event indicators or details.

1. Go to the Calendar app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1b944caed2601c11a4cfa936cfd46342.png) on your iPhone.

2. In Month view, tap the Month view icon, then do one of the following:

   - _Show a single bar indicator for the events on each day:_ Tap Compact ![the Compact button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/384fdba93f8ed6281c4442e2b0a9c605.png).

   - _Show a bar indicator for each event:_ Tap Stacked ![the Stacked button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1a4c4e0d41eb07cd7a69e9fe0cb3e376.png).

   - _Show event titles:_ Tap Details ![the Details button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b612271327c5a824727ded9ceb427fc8.png).
