# Add backgrounds in Messages on iPhone

You can add a background to an iMessage conversation. Other people in the conversation see the background on their devices too and can change the background for the group.

_Note:_ To add, view, or change conversation backgrounds, you must use iMessage on iOS 26, iPadOS 26, macOS Tahoe, watchOS 26, visionOS 26, or later.

![A Messages conversation with a background.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9704d790abc952ddc2dcd86f578dafe9.png)

## Use a dynamic background

1. Go to the Messages app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ad1f1e874b79cd34473c266e98f9cbd0.png) on your iPhone.

2. Go to the conversation where you want to add a background.

3. Tap the contact or group icon at the top of the conversation, then tap Backgrounds.

4. Tap Color, Sky, Water, or Aurora.

5. Swipe left to choose an option.

**Tip:** For Color, tap the swatches in the bottom corners to adjust the colors.

6. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7c29db565673f106ec2610dbf4a90268.png) to add the background to your conversation.
