## Change the music of a memory movie

You can change a memory movie’s music by choosing from memory soundtracks or suggested songs in Apple Music. Apple Music subscribers can also access their Apple Music library and view suggested songs based on musical preferences or the content of the memory.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Swipe left under Memories to scroll through a selection of your memories, or tap Memories to view them all.

4. Tap ![the Play button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de4289510e31551c9b519237047a4ca.png) in the bottom-right corner of a memory to play it.

5. Tap the screen, then tap ![the Memory Mixes button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/309496ba56eb4ffc1549edc5e4a84a32.png).

6. Tap ![the Music button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f7ebafec367305e17c1b03b90cf50e4a.png), tap a song, then tap Done.

Apple Music subscribers can tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png) to search for songs in the Apple Music library.
