## Add or remove photos in a memory movie

You can choose the photos that appear in a memory movie—add or remove suggested photos, or choose photos and videos directly from your library.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Swipe left under Memories to scroll through a selection of your memories, or tap Memories to view them all.

4. Tap ![the Play button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de4289510e31551c9b519237047a4ca.png) in the bottom-right corner of a memory to play it.

5. Tap the screen, then tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8a1f0a8df86eb1c80021954b0f092e6e.png).

6. Tap Manage Photos, then do any of the following:

   - _Add a photo from your photo library:_ Tap All, scroll through your library, select the photos you want to add, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

   - _Remove photos:_ Deselect a photo to remove it from the memory, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

   - _Search for photos in your photo library:_ Tap All, tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png), then type the name of a person, place, or date. Tap a search result, select the photos you want to add, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).
7. Tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6a2ce9f8f356d0a4dcdcfc384ef38ef9.png) to close the memory movie.
