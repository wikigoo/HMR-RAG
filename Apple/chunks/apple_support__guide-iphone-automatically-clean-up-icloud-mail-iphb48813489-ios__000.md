# Automatically clean up your iCloud Mail inbox on iPhone

You can use iCloud Mail Cleanup to help keep your inbox uncluttered and free up storage by deleting old promotions, unsubscribing from mailing lists, and more. When you set up iCloud Mail Cleanup recommendations, they work on any device that has iCloud Mail turned on.

_Note:_ iCloud Mail Cleanup may not be available in all languages.

## Turn on iCloud Mail Cleanup

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap \[ _your name_\], then tap iCloud.

3. Below Saved to iCloud, tap Mail, tap iCloud Mail Cleanup, then turn on Use iCloud Mail Cleanup.

_Note:_ Your inbox isn’t affected until you turn on a cleanup recommendation.

You can also turn on iCloud Mail Cleanup on iCloud.com. See [Automatically clean up your iCloud Mail inbox on iCloud.com](https://support.apple.com/guide/icloud/mma3d15ee93e/).

## Turn on a cleanup recommendation

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap \[ _your name_\], then tap iCloud.

3. Below Saved to iCloud, tap Mail, then tap iCloud Mail Cleanup.

4. Tap Get Recommendation under one of the cleanup recommendations. For example, you can choose Clean up Transactions or Clean Up Promotions.

5. If necessary, tap the pop-up menu for a rule, then choose another option.

   - _Action:_ What happens to the email

   - _Status:_ Whether the action applies to unread emails, read emails, or all emails

   - _Age:_ How old the emails need to be before the action applies

**Tip:** You can edit the rules for a recommendation at any point. Tap an active recommendation, then tap a pop-up menu to choose an option.

6. Tap Turn On.

## Delete a cleanup recommendation

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap \[ _your name_\], then tap iCloud.

3. Below Saved to iCloud, tap Mail, then tap iCloud Mail Cleanup.

4. Tap an active cleanup recommendation, then turn it off.
