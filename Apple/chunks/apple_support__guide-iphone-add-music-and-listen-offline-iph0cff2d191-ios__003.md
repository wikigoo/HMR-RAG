## Manage storage space

To manage storage space, do any of the following:

- _Remove music and videos stored on iPhone:_ Go to the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) on your iPhone, touch and hold an item that you downloaded, then tap Remove Download.

The item is removed from iPhone, but not from your music library on your other devices.

- _Free up music storage:_ If iPhone is low on storage space, you can automatically remove downloaded music that you haven’t played in a while. Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music > Optimize Storage.

- _Remove all songs or certain artists from iPhone:_ Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music > Downloaded Music, tap Edit, then tap ![the Delete button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f49364d4831a585edf6235c7a73db060.png) next to All Songs or the artists whose music you want to delete.

- _Remove all Apple Music songs from iPhone:_ Go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) \> Apps > Music, then turn off Sync Library.

The songs are removed from iPhone but remain in your music library on your other devices. Music you purchased or synced also remains.
