# Restart your iPad

Learn how to turn your iPad off, then back on.

## How to restart an iPad without a Home button

1. Press and hold either volume button and the top button until the power off slider appears.
![Pressing and holding the volume and top buttons on an iPad without a Home button until the power off slider appears.](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/ipad/ipad/restart-ipad-pro-face-id-ipad-mini.png)
2. Drag the slider, then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off. If your device is frozen or unresponsive, [force restart your device](https://support.apple.com/guide/ipad/restart-ipad-ipad63d30b5a/14.0/ipados/14.0#iPad715e3eec).

3. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.

## How to restart an iPad with a Home button

1. Press and hold the top button until the power off slider appears.
![Illustration of pressing the top button on an iPad without a home button to initiate the restart process.](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/ipad/ipad/restart-ipad-home-button.png)
2. Drag the slider, then wait for 30 seconds for your device to turn off. If your device is frozen or unresponsive, [force restart your device](https://support.apple.com/guide/ipad/restart-ipad-ipad63d30b5a/14.0/ipados/14.0#iPad715e3eec).

3. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.

## **How to restart an iPad in Control Center.**

In iOS 18 and later, you can turn off your device from Control Center.

1. Swipe down from the top-right corner of your screen to open Control Center.

2. Press and hold the Power button![](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/inline-icons/power-icon.png) in the top-right corner of your screen, then let go.
![Control center open on iPad with the power button icon highlighted.](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/ipados/ipados-26-ipad-pro-control-center-power-icon.png)
3. Drag the slider, then wait for 30 seconds for your device to turn off.

4. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.
