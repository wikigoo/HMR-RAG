# Restart your iPhone

Learn how to turn your iPhone off, then back on.

## How to restart your iPhone X, 11, 12, 13, or later

1. Press and hold either volume button and the side button until the power-off slider appears.
![Pressing the volume button on the left and the side button on the right initiates the power-off sequence.](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/iphone/iphone-x-and-later-restart.png)
2. Drag the slider, then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off. If your device is frozen or unresponsive, [force restart your device](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/force-restart-iphone-iph8903c3ee6/ios).

3. To turn your device back on, press and hold the side button (on the right side of your iPhone) until you see the Apple logo.


## How to restart your iPhone 6, 7, 8, or SE (2nd or 3rd generation)

1. Press and hold the side button until the power-off slider appears.
![Press and hold the side button on your iPhone as the first step to restart it.](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/iphone/iphone-6-and-earlier-restart.png)
2. Drag the slider, then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off. If your device is frozen or unresponsive, [force restart your device](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/force-restart-iphone-iph8903c3ee6/ios).

3. To turn your device back on, press and hold the side button until you see the Apple logo.


## How to restart your iPhone SE (1st generation), 5, or earlier

1. Press and hold the top button until the power-off slider appears.
![The power button on top of iPhone SE (1st generation), 5, or earlier](https://cdsassets.apple.com/live/7WUAS350/images/iphone/iphone-se-restart.png)
2. Drag the slider, then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off. If your device is frozen or unresponsive, [force restart your device](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/force-restart-iphone-iph8903c3ee6/ios).

3. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.
