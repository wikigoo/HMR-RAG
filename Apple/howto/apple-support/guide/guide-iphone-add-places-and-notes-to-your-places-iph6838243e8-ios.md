# Add places and notes to your Places in Maps on iPhone

You can find pins, places, guides, and routes in one place: your Places in the Maps app. Add your own personal notes—visible only to you—to saved places, or pin your most frequently visited places for easier access.

![The Places screen in Maps, which shows a list of place categories like Pinned, Saved Places, and Guides. Below this, a Recently Added section lists viewed locations.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/01ca83d2f97141ad6335d883f85f9d31.png)

## Save a place

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. While looking at a place card, do one of the following:

   - Tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0d07cb7185e2d87af8a9ad8b4ace6995.png). It turns to ![the Added button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ae89099ef98753805310a8a593327c52.png).

   - Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Add to Places.

## Add notes to a place

You can read the notes you add to a place at any time. When you share a place, your note appears as text with the shared link, but it doesn’t save to the other person’s notes for that place.

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. While looking at a place card, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Add a Note.

3. Write something, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).


Adding a note to a place saves the place to your Places.

## Add a pin to your Places

You can pin frequently visited places for easier access in Maps and CarPlay. They appear as icons in your Places and also under Pinned.

![The Maps app showing pins in Places and Recents.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/74c8150306fe66ed5f8c57714ec1bf5a.png)

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Below Places, tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5bed9fbc900ff3d17706314a2ff67f23.png) (you may need to swipe left), then search for or tap a suggestion. You can also [drop a pin](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/mark-a-location-with-a-pin-iphfa7286b57/26/ios/26#iphc4e0624cf).

3. If the pins are for your Home, Work, or School, you can do any of the following:

   - _Assign it to a type:_ Select Home, Work, or School.

   - _Sync to My Card:_ Tap Add to Contact Card.
4. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).


See [Edit a pin](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/mark-a-location-with-a-pin-iphfa7286b57/26/ios/26#iphc3ff17bb4).

## Remove a place from your Places

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Places.

3. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Delete From Places.


If you’re in the place card, tap ![the Added button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ae89099ef98753805310a8a593327c52.png). It returns to ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0d07cb7185e2d87af8a9ad8b4ace6995.png).

Removing a place from your Places deletes any personal [notes you added](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-places-and-notes-to-your-places-iph6838243e8/26/ios/26#iph128959ca1) to it.
