# Use contacts from the Phone app on iPhone

In the Phone app on iPhone, you can call contacts and add recent callers to the Contacts app.

## Add a contact to Favorites

You can add VIP contacts to your Favorites list for quick dialing.

1. Go to the Contacts app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/16b966e440ec5fb9278ce6235caabc74.png) on your iPhone.

2. Select a contact, scroll down, then tap Add to Favorites.


Calls from these contacts bypass Do Not Disturb (see [Turn on or schedule a Focus](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-a-focus-on-or-off-iph5c3f5b77b/26/ios/26)).

## Add a recent caller to Contacts

1. Go to the Phone app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4651af24ad17cb6ac594c96c6641ba6f.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap a number in the Calls list.

3. Tap Create New Contact, then enter the contact’s information.

To add a recent caller to an existing contact, tap Add to Existing Contact, then choose a contact.


## Automate dialing an extension or passcode

If the number you’re calling requires dialing an extension, iPhone can enter it for you. When editing a contact’s phone number, tap ![the Symbols button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/01291458f61f4700d8e72db61ac00121.png), then do any of the following:

- Tap Pause to enter a two-second pause (a two-second pause is represented as a comma in the phone number).

- Tap Wait to stop dialing until you tap Dial again (wait-to-dial is represented as a semicolon in the phone number).


**See also** [Add and use contact information on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-and-use-contact-information-iph3e0ca2db/26/ios/26)
