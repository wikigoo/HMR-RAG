# Use Apple Intelligence in Mail on iPhone

Apple Intelligence\* makes it easier than ever to stay on top of your email. In addition to [using Writing Tools](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/find-the-right-words-with-writing-tools-iph6f08da1d2/26/ios/26) to craft your outgoing messages, you can use Priority Messages to quickly view your most urgent emails, like a same-day dinner invitation or boarding pass. Smart Reply helps you compose a fast email response to make sure you covered everything. You can also view summaries that automatically appear in your inbox, and generate summaries of complex emails and long email threads to view all the pertinent details.

![An iPhone screen showing an email. A summary of the email appears at the top of the screen.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f570d145daf366e11a45454d34eb83cd.png)

_Note:_ Apple Intelligence is not available on all [iPhone models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph275f4d617/26/ios/26) or in all languages or regions.\*\* To access the most recent available features, make sure you’re using the [latest version of iOS](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/update_os/26/ios/26) and have [Apple Intelligence turned on](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph3212e4900/26/ios/26).

## View Priority Messages in your inbox

1. Go to the Mail app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/352a64cda8fca90aa29ad6f4f6185ee5.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap your inbox, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8a1f0a8df86eb1c80021954b0f092e6e.png), then tap Show Priority.

Time-sensitive emails appear at the top of your inbox.



_Note:_ Priority Messages appear in the Primary category or in List View.


## Use Smart Reply in Mail

1. Go to the Mail app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/352a64cda8fca90aa29ad6f4f6185ee5.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap an email, tap ![the Reply button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/54b80d14295606b13cd15c37e62e32de.png), then tap Reply.

3. In the keyboard, tap a suggested reply.

Apple Intelligence drafts a reply in the email. To refine your reply, see [Use Writing Tools with Apple Intelligence on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/find-the-right-words-with-writing-tools-iph6f08da1d2/26/ios/26).

4. When you’re ready to send your reply, tap ![the Send button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f5dabff8f401af1580540b6ab7aedcb8.png).


## Summarize your email

Apple Intelligence can help you summarize a complex email, or catch up on a long email thread in seconds.

1. Go to the Mail app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/352a64cda8fca90aa29ad6f4f6185ee5.png) on your iPhone.

A short summary automatically appears under each email in your inbox.

2. Tap an email, then tap Summarize.


A summary appears at the top of the screen. If the message is part of an email thread, the contents of the thread are summarized.

## Turn email summary previews on or off

When you turn on Apple Intelligence, summaries automatically appear under your emails. You can turn off summaries if you want to see the first lines of the most recent message instead.

1. Go to the Settings app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/610900798ec772fa266e98f9f6042807.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Apps, then tap Mail.

3. Turn Summarize Message Previews on or off.
