# Hide people, memories, and holidays in Photos on iPhone

You can set the Photos app to show certain people, days, and holidays less frequently or not at all in your memories, featured photos, and the Photos widget. You can also turn off memories and featured photos in the Photos app and the widget on your iPhone Home Screen.

## Show a person less frequently or not at all

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Open a photo of the person you want to show less often.

3. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/53cc5dafac91abd63db83c91b43ef920.png), then tap Don’t Feature a Person.

If there’s more than one person in the photo, tap the person you want to feature less, then tap Next.

4. Choose Feature This Person Less or Never Feature This Person.

5. Tap Confirm.


To feature someone again, tap Collections, tap your account in the top-right corner, then tap Reset People & Pets Suggestions.

## Turn off memories, holidays, and other featured content

You can turn off memories or featured photos in the Photos app, and prevent holiday events in your home country or region from appearing in memories and featured content.

1. Go to the Photos app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6653ad847aeffaa071397f0b0db8c03d.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Collections.

3. Tap your account in the top-right corner.

4. Do any of the following:

   - _Hide featured content and memories:_ Turn off Show Featured Content to prevent Photos from automatically generating featured photos and memories.

   - _Turn off holiday events:_ Turn off Show Holiday Events to prevent Photos from creating memories and featured content about holidays.

**See also** [Personalize your memory movies in Photos on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/personalize-your-memory-movies-iph1a5832438/26/ios/26) [Delete or hide photos and videos on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/delete-or-hide-photos-and-videos-iphb4defbde9/26/ios/26)
