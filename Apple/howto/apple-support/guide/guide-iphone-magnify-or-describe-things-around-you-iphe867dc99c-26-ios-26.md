# Magnify or describe things around you with Magnifier on iPhone

In the Magnifier app, you can use your iPhone like a magnifying glass to zoom in on text or objects around you.

![The Magnifier app showing a close-up of an unripe orange.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4f58a526cefccbeb7f1440dd5659e1e6.png)

## Magnify nearby text or objects

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Point the iPhone camera at the text or object you want to magnify, then drag the slider to zoom in or out.

3. You can also use any of the following controls:

   - Tap ![the Activities button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f819c7e25681484175550cdec5b01440.png) to switch between your default controls and custom sets of controls you’ve defined for recurring tasks or situations (called _activities_). See [Set your preferred controls for an activity](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-controls-ipheed8d2fca/26/ios/26#iphef1068634).

   - Tap ![the Freeze frame button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png) to [temporarily freeze the frame](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph2c76e5eaa).

   - Tap ![the Settings button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a3f742160163c000f2710f952646cafe.png) to [add more controls](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-controls-ipheed8d2fca/26/ios/26), including [Detection Mode](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph32deb9296), or edit activities you’ve created.

**Tip:** Set up a shortcut to quickly open Magnifier by clicking or pressing an iPhone button or tapping the back of iPhone, or from Control Center. See [Quickly turn accessibility features on or off](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/quickly-turn-accessibility-features-on-or-off-iph3e2e31a5/26/ios/26).

On [supported models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph345af6a5d/26/ios/26), you can also customize the Camera Control to open Magnifier. See [Use the Camera Control to open another app](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-the-camera-control-to-open-another-app-iph3940f00d2/26/ios/26).

## Temporarily freeze the frame

You can temporarily capture what’s in the frame, without saving it to Photos.

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png), then do one of the following:

   - _Take one or more photos:_ Press and hold ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png), then select Single Photo or Multi-Photo to take a photo or multiple photos.

     If you select Multi-Photo, you’ll see a group of photos in the bottom right corner of the screen.

   - _Capture text and reframe it:_ Press and hold ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png), then select Document. Follow the written and spoken instructions to get the text in frame or drag the corners of the box that appear around the text, then tap Confirm.

     After you tap Confirm, tap ![the Reader button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/66c227b33be6046ef0d5bae03157187b.png) to view the text with Accessibility Reader. See [view your captured text in a format for easier reading](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph9891f10ec) or to [listen to your captured text](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph092daeb86).

   - _Customize and listen to text:_ If you’re magnifying text, tap ![the Reader button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/66c227b33be6046ef0d5bae03157187b.png) to [view your captured text in a format for easier reading](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph9891f10ec) or to [listen to your captured text](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/magnify-or-describe-things-around-you-iphe867dc99c/26/ios/26#iph092daeb86).

   - _Share captured images:_ Tap ![the Share button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/748a151e2276ad5df4d03ec8d4506bf7.png) for more options, such as sharing the captured frame or saving it to your photos.

   - _Stop viewing captured images:_ Tap ![the Close button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4ed21f66913b5f929023d831b52cf74d.png) to unfreeze the frame and return to the live Magnifier lens.

To reset Magnifier, press and hold ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png) anytime and select Document, Multi-Photo, or Single-Photo.

## View your captured text in a format for easier reading

If you magnify a frame that contains text, you can view it in a format adapted for easier reading. You can even adjust fonts, layouts, and background colors.

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png) to capture the frame, then tap ![the Reader button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/66c227b33be6046ef0d5bae03157187b.png).

Magnifier alerts you if no text is detected in the frame you captured.

3. Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38f95724ed0a62ccb8a071d3df51b3b2.png), then tap Customize Reader.

4. Select a theme, then tap Edit.

5. Do any of the following:

   - _Text Color:_ Choose a color or tap Custom Color.

   - _Background Color:_ Choose a color or tap Custom Color.

   - _Text Size:_ Drag the slider left or right to make the text smaller or larger.

   - _Font:_ Choose a font.

   - _High Legibility Characters:_ Turn on High Legibility Characters.



     _Note:_ This option is only available when using System Font.

   - _Bold Text:_ Turn on Bold Text.

   - _Line Spacing:_ Choose ![the Default button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b9fd2d6aa9e92a26672acc6acc9327ab.png), ![the Loose button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/67cae9458848135e5e3a3dec03fe8940.png), or ![the Open button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/72c3dcb90d47f8f65dd0be433f35db6e.png) to customize the space between lines.

   - _Word Spacing:_ Choose ![the Default button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b53ea49e79f5070b4c8308ec966ed508.png), ![the Loose button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e8b94e5c8d74595eb33f4d73c084250c.png), or ![the Open button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1fcbaa4266cbefa684205b42217aa50c.png) to customize the space between words.

   - _Character Spacing:_ Choose ![the Default button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/514bdb16f8b93bad3df3861afce490bc.png), ![the Loose button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/34484e8b314237c6c51eaa2fc26d3a60.png), or ![the Open button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/75f9ecab7ddcfcf612ea9427dce92aa2.png) to customize the space between characters.

   - _Hyperlinks:_ Turn Show Links on or off. When turned on, you can customize the color.

   - _Playback:_ Turn Highlighting on or off. When turned on, you can customize the style or highlight color.

   - _Rename:_ Enter a name for the settings you choose.

   - _Reset to Default:_ Reset all themes to their default settings.



     **Tip:** Rename a theme with your custom settings so you can easily find it and reuse it later.

You can also use Accessibility Reader to customize the text in apps. See [Read or listen to text in apps with Accessibility Reader on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/read-listen-text-apps-accessibility-reader-iph406a46ab8/26/ios/26).

## Listen to your captured text

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Capture button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c790ddb8a706a9d6fd724ec5908b853f.png) to capture the frame, then tap ![the Reader button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/66c227b33be6046ef0d5bae03157187b.png).

Magnifier alerts you if no text is detected in the frame you captured.

3. Do any of the following:

   - _Listen to the text:_ Tap ![the Play button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9fc2474e8f975c92e61abda72cf78910.png).

   - _Pause the text:_ Tap ![the Pause button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a5f086eae69a48bd1373d00365edeef5.png).

   - _Skip backward or forward:_ Tap ![the Rewind button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9530a528673e0d50898c9ff1d9460c41.png) or ![the Forward button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e2ca2b5b7a76fb497f5ca74cc4fc2611.png).

   - _Increase or decrease the speed:_ Tap ![the Speaking Rate One Times button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/74f8bb5dd4579fbe396327e9f3c2478c.png), then select a speed.

   - _Go to a specific time:_ Drag the slider left or right.



     _Note:_ You can hide or show audio controls: Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38f95724ed0a62ccb8a071d3df51b3b2.png), then tap ![the Hide Audio Controls button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/24b79b52ef0508fdefc6154bd64e4384.png).

## Get live descriptions of your surroundings

If you’re blind or have low vision, you can use Detection Mode in the Magnifier app on iPhone to scan your surroundings and get live descriptions of the scenes detected in the camera view. Live descriptions are available as text or speech.

**Important:** Detection Mode should not be relied on in high-risk or emergency situations, in circumstances where you may be harmed or injured, or for navigation.

1. Go to the Magnifier app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/39aaaabcae6f66284764c8368a31216c.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![the Detection Mode button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/db975b0a1433f2f55117411860975371.png) to start Detection Mode.

If you don’t see ![the Detection Mode button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/db975b0a1433f2f55117411860975371.png), you can add it. See [Customize controls](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-controls-ipheed8d2fca/26/ios/26).

3. Touch and hold ![the Detection Mode button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/db975b0a1433f2f55117411860975371.png), select Scenes, then do any of the following:

   - On [supported iPhone models](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphc07537a89/26/ios/26), detect [doors, people, and furniture](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/detect-doors-people-and-furniture-around-you-iph35c335575/26/ios/26#iph1320e1f65) near you.

   - On all iPhone models, [detect text in the camera frame and have it read out loud](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/detect-text-read-loud-iph29dbe3fb6/26/ios/26#iph13d9d4f67) or [point your finger at text to have it spoken](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/detect-text-read-loud-iph29dbe3fb6/26/ios/26#iph11ad5bda8).
4. To temporarily pause detection, double-tap the screen with two fingers. Double-tap again with two fingers to resume detection. To stop Detection Mode, tap ![the Detection Mode button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/db975b0a1433f2f55117411860975371.png).


If you use VoiceOver, you can turn on Live Recognition from any screen on iPhone and get descriptions of your surroundings without going to the Magnifier app. See [Get live descriptions of your surroundings with VoiceOver](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/get-live-descriptions-of-your-surroundings-iph37e6b3844/26/ios/26).
