# Organize places with custom guides in Maps on iPhone

You can organize places into your own guides for easy reference, and you can share your guides with others.

![A list of several custom guides in Maps, including a Favorites category, and the New Guide button at the bottom right.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/79c71d23df4a2861e2cbc3a52407b9f9.png)

## See your favorites

A guide called Favorites always appears in your guides. This makes it easier for you to save places to a guide.

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Your Guides, then tap Favorites.


**Tip:** You can pin any places that you want to see in your Places when you open Maps. See [Add a pin to your Places](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-places-and-notes-to-your-places-iph6838243e8/26/ios/26#iphe5d4c6f40).

## Create a guide

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Your Guides, then tap ![the Add to List button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/058005053f0d2261b9f5c7e7a50141c5.png).

3. Enter a name for the guide, then tap ![the Add Image button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/63b6ec96d982c274cb4c9a812fc89c48.png) to add an image from your photo library (optional).

4. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).


## Add a place to a guide

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap a place on the map or a search result.

3. In the place card, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png), then tap Add to Guides.

4. Choose one of your guides, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).


You can also add Publisher Guides to your guides. See [Explore places with guides](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/explore-places-with-guides-iph4c213d62d/26/ios/26).

## Share a guide

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Your Guides, tap the guide you want to share.

3. Tap ![the Share button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/748a151e2276ad5df4d03ec8d4506bf7.png), then choose an option.


## Edit a guide that you created

For any guide that you create, you can supply a cover image, change the title, and remove places.

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Your Guides, then tap the guide you want to edit.

3. Tap ![the Edit button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e8c6ffaa6db04ef3fbeca71a9b0b0ecc.png), make your changes, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).


## Remove a guide

1. Go to the Maps app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/5c95fac6a0912a04a330864acfcec1fb.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Your Guides, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/aad05ec99d91fb0497859fc9c0c181fb.png) on the guide you want to remove, then tap Delete from Places.
