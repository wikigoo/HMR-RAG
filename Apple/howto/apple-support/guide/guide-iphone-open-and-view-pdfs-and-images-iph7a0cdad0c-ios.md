# Open and view PDFs and images in Preview on iPhone

You can use the Preview app to open and view PDF documents and images.

![The main screen of the Preview app, showing PDFs and images. At the bottom of the screen are the Recents, Shared, and Browse tabs.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/843a37f313390e42400d280036d8dd4d.png)

## Open a PDF or image

You can browse and open PDFs and images directly in Preview, without opening the Files app first.

1. Go to the Preview app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ca7f2c2acd3368373c4d43c309574cf1.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following to find a file:

   - _View files you opened recently:_ Tap Recents.

   - _View shared files:_ Tap Shared.

   - _Browse available files:_ Tap Browse.

   - _Choose a location to browse:_ Tap Browse, tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ea56b8edd3ca92d7af8adc2abea59bd5.png), then tap an option under Locations.

   - _Browse by tag:_ Tap Browse, tap ![the Back button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ea56b8edd3ca92d7af8adc2abea59bd5.png), then tap an option under Tags.
3. Optionally, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38f95724ed0a62ccb8a071d3df51b3b2.png), then select how items appear (icons or list) or how items are sorted.

4. Tap a file to open it.


**Tip:** If someone emails you a PDF form to fill out, you can tap the attached file in the Mail app to open and complete the form in Preview. You can then reply to the sender and attach the completed form. See [Reply to an email with a PDF attachment](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/reply-to-an-email-with-a-pdf-attachment-iph5be45d7f0/26/ios/26).

## View a PDF or image

1. Go to the Preview app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ca7f2c2acd3368373c4d43c309574cf1.png) on your iPhone.

2. [Open the PDF or image](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/open-and-view-pdfs-and-images-iph7a0cdad0c/26/ios/26#iph47b7f742f) you want to view.

3. Do any of the following:

   - _Zoom in or out:_ Pinch two fingers open or closed on a page or image.

   - _Show thumbnails for a PDF:_ Tap the page count. (If the page count doesn’t appear, tap the screen.)

   - _Hide thumbnails for a PDF:_ Pinch two fingers open on a page until the thumbnails disappear.

   - _Scroll pages in a PDF:_ Swipe up or down.

   - _Go to a specific page in a PDF:_ Tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/38f95724ed0a62ccb8a071d3df51b3b2.png), then tap Go to Page. Enter a page number, then tap Go.

## Find text in a PDF

1. Go to the Preview app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ca7f2c2acd3368373c4d43c309574cf1.png) on your iPhone.

2. [Open a PDF](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/open-and-view-pdfs-and-images-iph7a0cdad0c/26/ios/26#iph47b7f742f) you want to search.

3. Tap ![the Search button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/3506e345e882236df240ce6ef039212b.png).

4. Optionally, tap ![the Search Options button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/bc9621b70009a1ef45b0454cc056116e.png) in the search field and do any of the following:

   - _Exclude matches where the search text is part of another word:_ Select Whole Word.

   - _Only include results that match the capitalization of the search text:_ Select Match Case.
5. Enter a word or phrase in the search field.

If you enter a phrase, Preview searches for that exact phrase.

6. Tap ![the Next Result button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/d9ef375b37f3e012ba5381d26fd9d6a8.png) or ![the Previous Result button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b63d64e291d5e3d92005d1e487609e13.png) to go to the next or previous search result.

7. Tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7c29db565673f106ec2610dbf4a90268.png) to close the search results.


## View information about a PDF or image

You can get information about PDF documents and images, such as creation date, filename, and more. When location information is available for a photo, you can also view the latitude and longitude where the photo was taken.

1. Go to the Preview app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ca7f2c2acd3368373c4d43c309574cf1.png) on your iPhone.

2. [Open a PDF or image](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/open-and-view-pdfs-and-images-iph7a0cdad0c/26/ios/26#iph47b7f742f).

3. Tap ![the Info button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/681b1fd7f3f687b717fd1b699fcc723c.png).
