# Make a call on iPhone

There are a number of ways you can make a phone call on your iPhone: dial the number on the keypad, tap a favorite or recent call, or choose a number in your contacts list.

![The Calls tab in the Phone app in Unified layout. Favorite contacts appear at the top. Below the Favorites is a list of recent calls and voicemail. Buttons to view the Calls, Contacts, and Keypad tabs appear at the bottom. The Search button is in the bottom-right corner.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4928857c56295cf9a514caafdb9a3053.png)

## Dial a number

**Siri:** [Ask Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/summon_siri/26/ios/26) to call or dial a number. Speak each digit separately—for example, “four one five, five five five….” For the 800 area code in the U.S., you can say “eight hundred.” [Learn how to use Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/how-to-use-siri-ipha48873ed6/26/ios/26).

Or without using Siri:

1. Go to the Phone app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4651af24ad17cb6ac594c96c6641ba6f.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Keypad at the bottom of the screen, then do any of the following:

   - _Use a different line:_ On [models with Dual SIM](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphb1759eccd/26/ios/26), tap the line at the top, then choose a line.

   - _Enter the number using the keypad:_ If you make a mistake, tap ![the Delete button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/bcba02d7aaf21d0066f1fa548a494d02.png).

   - _Redial the last number:_ Tap ![the Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ccd56b2c36a80b69e63079a6319c6690.png) to see the last number you dialed, then tap ![the Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ccd56b2c36a80b69e63079a6319c6690.png) to call that number.

   - _Paste a number you’ve copied:_ Tap the phone number field above the keypad, then tap Paste.

   - _Enter a soft (2-second) pause:_ Touch and hold the star (\*) key until a comma appears.

   - _Enter a hard pause (to pause dialing until you tap the Dial button):_ Touch and hold the pound (#) key until a semicolon appears.

   - _Enter a “+” for international calls:_ Touch and hold the “0” key until “+” appears.
3. Tap ![the Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ccd56b2c36a80b69e63079a6319c6690.png) to start the call.


To end the call, tap ![the End Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e5dd68f3f3e1af9bde771d8cd250f866.png).

## Add or remove contacts from your Favorites

1. Go to the Phone app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4651af24ad17cb6ac594c96c6641ba6f.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do one of the following, depending on which [Phone app layout](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph74958a4e6/26/ios/26) you’re using:

   - _Unified layout:_ Tap Calls at the bottom of the screen, tap Edit, then tap Add Favorite. If you’ve already added a Favorite, tap Edit, tap Edit Favorites, then tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2c6dba0cb486a1dee023b7a6ce137565.png).

   - _Classic layout:_ Tap Favorites at the bottom of the screen, then tap ![the Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2c6dba0cb486a1dee023b7a6ce137565.png).
3. Tap a contact, choose the default method of communication (for example, message, call, or video) and the preferred number, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

4. To remove a contact from your Favorites, do one of the following:

   - _Unified layout:_ Tap Calls at the bottom of the screen, tap Edit, tap Edit Favorites, tap ![the Remove button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6047ad296fd44fcc70419598cf3da8fa.png) in the upper-left corner of the contact you want to remove, then tap ![the Done button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8cf38aa668e293079ba389088a78ec31.png).

   - _Classic layout:_ Tap Favorites at the bottom of the screen, then swipe their name all the way to the left.

To contact someone in your Favorites, tap their name.

## Redial or return a recent call

1. Go to the Phone app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4651af24ad17cb6ac594c96c6641ba6f.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do one of the following, depending on which [Phone app layout](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph74958a4e6/26/ios/26) you’re using:

   - _Unified layout:_ Tap Calls at the bottom of the screen, tap the name or number you want to call, tap ![the Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b7501530b3cfc58f2be0f17b95f30b72.png), then tap the number you want to call.



     _Note:_ To start a call as soon as you tap a name or number in the Recents list, go to Settings ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/f6b5581fdf38013a42e265720f20b597.png) \> Apps > Phone, then turn on Tap Recents to Call.

   - _Classic layout:_ Tap Recents at the bottom of the screen, then tap the name or number you want to call.
3. To get more info about a call and the caller, do one of the following:

   - _Unified layout:_ Tap the name or number in the Recents list.

   - _Classic layout:_ Tap ![the Info button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/c365a2ba2af247ead4528d734ba840f4.png) next to the name or phone number in Recents.

## Call someone on your contacts list

**Siri:** [Ask Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/summon_siri/26/ios/26) something like, “Call Eliza’s mobile.” [Learn how to use Siri](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/turn-on-and-activate-siri-iph83aad8922/26/ios/26).

Or without using Siri:

1. Go to the Phone app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4651af24ad17cb6ac594c96c6641ba6f.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap Contacts at the bottom of the screen, then tap the contact you want to call.

3. Tap ![the Call button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/b7501530b3cfc58f2be0f17b95f30b72.png), then tap the phone number you want to call.

On [models with Dual SIM](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iphb1759eccd/26/ios/26), the default voice line is used for the call unless you set a preferred line for this contact.
