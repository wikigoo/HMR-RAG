# What’s new in iOS 26

**Lock Screen.** The time dynamically adapts its position based on your Lock Screen photo or wallpaper and always keeps the subject in view. When you move your iPhone, your Lock Screen photo comes to life with a new 3D effect. See [Create a custom iPhone Lock Screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-a-custom-lock-screen-iph4d0e6c351/26/ios/26).

![The iPhone Lock Screen.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/22e149f75fab72b818e2bb626c6c986a.png)

**Home Screen.** App icons on your Home Screen have a new look that you can tint with different colors or turn light, dark, or clear. See [Customize apps and widgets on the iPhone Home Screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-apps-and-widgets-on-the-home-screen-iph385473442/26/ios/26).

![The iPhone Home Screen with tinted app icons.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/85ab08a62030702fec17ed3a4ad33295.png)

**Apple Intelligence.\*** Create custom Genmoji using existing emoji, combinations of emoji, or descriptive text. Change the look and expressions of Genmoji and Image Playground creations that are based on people from your Photos library. Design custom backgrounds for your Messages conversations. Live Translation automatically translates texts in Messages, shows live translated captions in FaceTime calls, and provides spoken translations for Phone calls. Use visual intelligence to learn more about what you’re viewing on your iPhone screen. See [Create original images with Image Playground on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-original-images-with-image-playground-iph0063238b5/26/ios/26), [Create your own emoji with Genmoji on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/create-your-own-emoji-with-genmoji-iph4e76f5667/26/ios/26), [Create and edit original backgrounds with Image Playground](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-apple-intelligence-in-messages-iph64709c5c3/26/ios/26#iph4452ecda1), [Translate messages, calls, and conversations on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/translate-messages-calls-and-conversations-iph22b72984d/26/ios/26), and [Learn more about the content on your iPhone screen](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-visual-intelligence-iph12eb1545e/26/ios/26#iph4d370546c).

![A phone call in the Phone app showing a conversation translated from German to English.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/1c2fd16f1965495585825ad9e0c115b4.png)

**Camera.** A simplified design highlights Photo and Video modes for quick access, while the other camera modes are just a swipe away. Camera controls are now grouped together onscreen, making it easier to adjust your camera settings in the moment. See [Switch between camera modes](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/camera-basics-iph263472f78/26/ios/26#iphac6618d52) and [Use iPhone camera tools to set up your shot](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/set-up-your-shot-iph3dc593597/26/ios/26).

![The Camera in Photo mode. At the bottom of the screen are buttons for the following camera controls: Flash, Live, Timer, Exposure, Styles, and Aspect.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a26844541dfb79fc497d82164d0d95df.png)

**Phone.** Remove unwanted distractions from unknown numbers with Call Screening, which gathers the necessary information before your phone rings, so you can make an informed decision on whether to pick up, decline, or ask for more information. Use Hold Assist to wait on hold for you and then send you a notification when it’s time to return to the call. See [Screen and block calls on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/screen-and-block-calls-iphe4b3f7823/26/ios/26) and [Receive a notification when you’re no longer on hold](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/while-on-a-call-iph3c9951d7/26/ios/26#iph03769ccb5).

![The Phone app shows a call in progress on the iPhone screen. In the middle of the screen is a notification asking whether to hold the call and be notified when to pick up. Within the notification is a Hold button and a Close button.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/7dc43f4995345692f30e34a5efe8b8c0.png)

**Messages.** Messages from unknown numbers are filtered into a separate area of your conversation list for you to review and approve. Backgrounds let you personalize conversations for everyone to enjoy using dynamic backgrounds, your own photos, or backgrounds you create in Image Playground. In group conversations, you can create polls to help make decisions, send money using Apple Cash, and see typing indicators. See [Screen and filter text messages on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/screen-and-filter-texts-iph203ab0be4/26/ios/26), [Add backgrounds in Messages on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-backgrounds-iph605fa06e4/26/ios/26), [Send, request, and receive payments with Apple Cash in Messages](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-apple-cash-iph385cf0980/26/ios/26#iphb7f1ac4be), and [Poll people in Messages on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/poll-people-in-a-conversation-iphde1787df4/26/ios/26).

![A Messages conversation with a dynamic background.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/9704d790abc952ddc2dcd86f578dafe9.png)

**Photos.** An updated design makes it easier to switch between your Library and Collections. You can now customize how Collections appear—reorder them, choose which ones to pin, and select from three display sizes: larger tiles, a denser grid, or a layout that highlights the top row. See [Browse your photo library on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/browse-your-photo-library-iph7d24753a5/26/ios/26) and [Browse your photo collections on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/browse-your-photo-collections-iph4f36c4148/26/ios/26).

![The Library screen in the Photos app.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/6c0c1af2cf439f969cee40af356f077a.png)

**Maps.** Maps learns your preferred routes to places you go often, like work or home. Maps will notify you of conditions on your preferred route, such as traffic delays or road closures, before you head out and offer you an alternate route. Use Visited Places to keep track of where you’ve been, so you can recall them later and share with friends or family. See [Get driving directions in Maps on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/get-driving-directions-ipha84a94043/26/ios/26) and [View and manage Visited Places in Maps on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-and-manage-visited-places-iph0c2597c4f/26/ios/26).

![The Visited Places screen in the Maps app.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/e3c93ede889cda47551045ff0c7ff716.png)

**Games.** A new app where you can keep track of all the games you’re playing, discover new ones, receive tailored recommendations, and start challenges with friends. See [Get started with the Apple Games app on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/get-started-with-the-apple-games-app-iph3aed54d2e/26/ios/26).

![The Apple Games app showing the Home tab, which displays a special event, games to continue playing, and new releases.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/158c11dce5b010c741943e50bf3ccc72.png)

**Audio recording.** Capture high-quality audio and video during video conference calls and with Camera. A new input chooser makes it easy for creators to pick the right microphone for each app. See [Record audio and video on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/record-audio-and-video-iph8dc8a5c3c/26/ios/26).

![The controls for choosing an audio input source for a FaceTime call.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/41fed1b05de4675194334c3ce27d487e.png)

**Music.** AutoMix seamlessly mixes one song to the next, just like a DJ. See [Adjust the sound quality in Music on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/adjust-the-sound-quality-iph5643d2c85/26/ios/26).

**Reminders.** (iOS 26.2) Mark a reminder as urgent to set an alarm that alerts you when it’s due. See [Add details in Reminders on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-details-iphec7a1de82/26/ios/26).

**Wallet.** Boarding passes that you save in Wallet provide access to relevant information about your flight and airport, as well as airline app features. See [Use passes in Wallet on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-passes-loyalty-cards-tickets-and-more-iphe7aa3336/26/ios/26).

![A boarding pass in the Wallet app, showing flight information and the QR code at the bottom.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/92d772c887abc0d2bf8ba26d722cab7d.png)

**Digital ID.** (iOS 26.1) In Wallet, create a Digital ID—a passport-verified ID that you can present at select TSA checkpoints in airports for domestic travel, or in supported apps or websites that require age verification. See [Add a Digital ID (U.S. only)](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-your-id-iph9f1847064/26/ios/26#iph3a659f49e).

**Preview.** The Preview app is now available on iPhone so you can easily view, edit, and share PDF documents and images. Scan documents from within Preview, use Autofill in Preview to quickly fill out forms, and export PDFs and images as different file types or sizes. See [Get started with Preview on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/get-started-with-preview-on-iphone-iph7239ea3b5/26/ios/26).

![The Document Browser in Preview. In the middle of the screen are the New Document, Scan Documents, More, and Search buttons. At the bottom of the screen are the Recents, Shared, and Browse buttons.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/843a37f313390e42400d280036d8dd4d.png)

**Clock.** Set a custom snooze time for your alarm to match your preferred sleeping habits. See [Set an alarm](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/set-an-alarm-iph2909d3a74/26/ios/26#iph600d94a8a).

![The Add Alarm screen in the Clock app. The Snooze is turned on and the Snooze Duration is set to a custom time of 12 minutes.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/8a2716ede5bacd60b4cbed023845f2b4.png)

**Accessibility.** Braille Access introduces an all-new experience designed for Braille users with a connected Braille display that enables note-taking, document-reading, access to Live Captions and more. Vehicle Motion Cues help reduce motion sickness for passengers in moving vehicles. You can also temporarily use your Accessibility settings on someone else’s device. See [Use Braille Access with a braille display on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-braille-access-with-a-braille-display-iph5953d3c7a/26/ios/26), [Use iPhone more comfortably while riding in a vehicle](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/iphone-comfortably-riding-a-vehicle-iph55564cb22/26/ios/26), and [Share your Accessibility settings with another device on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/share-accessibility-settings-device-ipha21bc67e7/26/ios/26).

![An iPhone with the Braille Access menu open, showing the following options: Launch App, Choose Item, Braille Notes, BRF Files, Calculator, Live Captions, and Time.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/4d66ea32783f6c9343bf46af395e7a8f.png)

**Family.** During device set up, parents can specify if they are setting up a device for an older child, age 13–17, and age-appropriate protections are automatically enabled. See [Share age ranges with apps on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/share-age-ranges-with-apps-iphe56aab53d/26/ios/26).

![The onboarding screen for Age-Appropriate Experiences in Apps features.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a9609e0ba89ee206a7f8554b37c25699.png)

**Journal.** Create multiple journals for different aspects of your life. As you write, include drawings inline with text, and then view the location of your journal entries on a map. See [Create multiple journals](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/write-in-your-journal-iph9824e83ce/26/ios/26#iph025e225ef), [Add formatting, photos, and more in Journal on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/add-formatting-photos-and-more-iph492ee70a8/26/ios/26), and [View and search your journal entries on iPhone](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-and-search-journal-entries-iph6257be047/26/ios/26).

![The Journal app showing a journal entry. At the top of the entry is a grid which includes multiple photos and a map.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2aa68335cc45ce470339aa14b41b975d.png)

**CarPlay.** New design makes CarPlay an easier and safer way to use your iPhone while you drive. Tapbacks in Messages make it easy to quickly respond to messages you receive while driving. Widgets let you keep up with your favorite apps at a glance and Live Activities on the dashboard let you keep track of events in real time. See [React with Tapbacks](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/send-and-receive-text-messages-iphb5d45624c/26/ios/26#iphcb39496c2), [Add a widget for CarPlay](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/customize-widgets-iphb4d6a0bbb/26/ios/26#iph252139641), and [View Live Activities in CarPlay](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/view-live-activities-iph3d92302ee/26/ios/26).

**Weather.** Add a Weather widget to your Smart Stack for an upcoming destination. Opt-in to receive severe weather alerts for that location. See [Get weather notifications for a suggested location](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/manage-weather-notifications-iph39ae9474a/26/ios/26#iphdf5879d46).

**Learn tricks for iOS 26.** The Tips app adds new suggestions regularly, so you can get the most from your iPhone. See [Get tips](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/tips-iph3afc3b3fc/26/ios/26).

_Note:_ New features and apps may vary depending on your iPhone model, region, language, and carrier.
