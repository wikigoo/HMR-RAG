# Start watching and control playback in the Apple TV app on iPhone

In the Apple TV app, you can stream TV shows, movies, sports, and more. You can also download movies and episodes or a full season of a TV show.

Content from Apple TV and Apple TV channels plays in the Apple TV app, while content from other providers plays in their video app. Use the playback controls to play or pause what you’re watching, skip backward or forward, and more.

When you watch with your friends using SharePlay, the playback controls are shared by everyone on the FaceTime call. To learn more about using SharePlay in the Apple TV app, see [Use SharePlay to watch, listen, and play together in FaceTime](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/shareplay-watch-listen-play-iphb657eb791/26/ios/26).

## Play a TV show or movie

![The Home tab showing a featured Apple Original in the center, above the Continue Watching row. At the bottom, from left to right, are the Home, Apple TV, Store, Library, and Search tabs.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/08e5599a6c8e1fc14d6a2dbdc34f8f59.png)[Stream _The Buccaneers_ on the Apple TV app with a subscription](https://tv.apple.com/us/show/the-buccaneers/umc.cmc.g28hcpusm23xbs9zwvnwn9re)

1. Go to the Apple TV app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/abed9ba23a400d9fcb746e6d2e1c3a59.png) on your iPhone.

2. Do any of the following:

   - Swipe left or right at the top of the screen to browse featured items, then tap the Play button (if available).

   - Tap Home, then tap an item in the [Continue Watching](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/personalize-the-home-tab-iphbf1137ac0/26/ios/26#iph97c4169ed) row to immediately pick up where you left off.

   - Scroll down to view more rows, then tap an item you want to watch. When the page for that item opens, tap the Play button (if available) or tap a specific episode or trailer you want to watch.



     **Tip:** Tap a row title to view all items in a category or view more information about a collection.

## Download content

![The Apple TV tab showing a list of episodes of an Apple Original. Buttons to download the entire season and each episode appear on the right side of the screen.](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2d689bf9343c1943d9fd1175176a58f5.png)[Stream _Hijack_ on the Apple TV app with a subscription](https://tv.apple.com/us/show/hijack/umc.cmc.1dg08zn0g3zx52hs8npoj5qe3)

In the Apple TV app, you can download movies and episodes of a TV show. You can also download a full season of a TV show.

1. Go to the Apple TV app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/abed9ba23a400d9fcb746e6d2e1c3a59.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap an item to see its details.

3. Do any of the following (not all options are available for all titles):

   - _Download movies:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner.

   - _Download an episode of a TV show:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner, tap a season, then tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a2bc1b532bdbca3dd86ef6d8380c53e0.png) next to the episode you want to download.

   - _Download a full season of a TV show:_ Tap ![the Download button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/96cc67f38140da9b3bb0e8f3dcedcb2e.png) in the top-right corner, tap a season, then tap the season button above the list of episodes.

You can [find the downloaded item in your library](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/manage-your-library-iph3ecbb37ad/26/ios/26) and watch it even when iPhone isn’t connected to the internet.

## Change audio options during playback

1. During playback, tap the screen to show the playback controls.

2. Tap ![the Audio Track button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/ebd585f0d6bc186a983b8830cfd99f24.png), then tap Enhance Dialogue.

3. Tap one of the following:

   - _Enhance More:_ Makes dialogue very prominent in the mix.

   - _Enhance:_ Makes dialogue more prominent in the mix.

   - _Off:_ Turns Enhance Dialogue off.

## Get information about shows, characters, actors, and music

During playback, you can get more information about the movie or TV show you’re watching or go directly to Continue Watching.

With InSight, you can get information in real time about actors, characters, and music currently onscreen while you’re watching an Apple TV movie or TV show (Apple TV subscription required). You can tap an actor to view their background and filmography page, or quickly view a song that’s playing in the background and add it to an Apple Music playlist (Apple Music subscription required).

1. During playback, tap the screen to show the playback controls.

2. Tap any of the following options:

   - _Info:_ See more information about what you’re watching.

   - _InSight:_ Tap an actor or character to see more info. If music information appears, tap the song to add it to a playlist in the Music app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a8584141e5732119d6e19d28e0fbd248.png) (Apple TV and Apple Music subscriptions required).

   - _Continue Watching:_ Go directly to Continue Watching, then tap an item to start watching it.

   - _Live Sports:_ View live and upcoming sports events.
