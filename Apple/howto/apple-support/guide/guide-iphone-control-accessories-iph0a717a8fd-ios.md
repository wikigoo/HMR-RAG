# Control accessories with Home on iPhone

Use the Home app and Control Center to control accessories in your home.

## Control accessories in the Home app

On the Home tab, tap an accessory’s icon on the left side of the tile—a light, for example—to quickly turn the accessory on or off. Tap the accessory’s name on the right side of the tile to show the accessory’s control.

The available controls depend on the type of accessory. For example, with some lightbulbs, there are controls for changing colors. With your smart TV, you can choose an input source.

## Add accessories to Control Center

You can add Home scenes and accessories to Control Center for easy access to your most used accessories.

1. [Open Control Center](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/aside/iph7c412b4db/26/ios/26) on your iPhone.

2. Tap ![The Add button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0d07cb7185e2d87af8a9ad8b4ace6995.png) in the top left, then tap Add A Control at the bottom.

3. Tap the Home Scene or Accessory control.

4. Tap Choose Scene or Accessory, then select a home and device or scene you want to control.


## View categories and home status

The Lights, Climate, Security, Speakers and TVs, and Water categories let you quickly access all the relevant accessories organized by room.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap the Home tab.

Below your home’s name, buttons show the status of accessories belonging to a category—for example, a Lights category that shows “3 on.”

3. Tap one of the category buttons to show all accessories within that category, organized by room.

4. While viewing a category, tap the icon on the left side of an accessory tile to perform an action—turn a light or group of lights on or off, start playing music on a HomePod, or lock the front door.


## View Activity History

When you have a home hub using the [new version of Apple Home](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/upgrade-to-the-new-version-of-apple-home-iphaeb0ce2d3/26/ios/26) and latest software, all the members of your home (except guests) can view the activity history for accessories like garage doors, locks, security systems, and [adaptive temperature](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/adaptive-temperature-clean-energy-guidance-iphdf6fde3d8/26/ios/26#iph8828aa73f) thermostats. For example, you can learn when a door was unlocked and who unlocked it.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. On the Home tab, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de35ce63d633769b9bfeb184c874452.png), then tap Home Settings.

3. Go to Activity, then tap One Month to view up to 30 days of activity.

4. In the Home tab, tap the Security or Climate category, then tap Home Activity to show the Activity History.


## Turn Activity History on and off

Only the home owner or a shared home user with permission to add and edit accessories can turn Activity History on and off. When you turn off Activity History, all previous activity for the home is permanently deleted.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. On the Home tab, tap ![the More button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/2de35ce63d633769b9bfeb184c874452.png), tap Home Settings, then tap Activity.

3. Choose One Month to turn Activity History on, or choose Off.


## Turn on Adaptive Lighting

Some Apple Home-compatible lights let you adjust their color temperature, from cool blue to warm yellow. You can set supported lights to automatically adjust the color temperature throughout the day. Wake up to warm colors, stay alert and focused midday with cooler ones, and wind down at night by removing blue light. For a light that supports Adaptive Lighting, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap the light’s tile to show the controls.

You can also touch and hold the tile, and then choose Accessory Settings.

3. Tap ![the Adaptive Lighting button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/0de000aca1fb89d0163de51e703b0455.png), then turn on Adaptive Lighting.


_Note:_ Adaptive Lighting requires a home hub—a device such as Apple TV 4K or HomePod speakers.

## Edit home accessories

To edit accessory settings, tap the right side of an accessory tile, tap ![the Settings button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a3f742160163c000f2710f952646cafe.png), then do any of the following:

- _Rename an accessory:_ Tap ![the Delete button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/14ad7e04408a158fcb2ba32511cfe8cf.png) to delete the old name, then type a new one.

- _Change an accessory’s icon:_ Tap the icon next to the accessory’s name, then select a new icon. If you don’t get a choice of other icons, it means the icon can’t be changed for this accessory.


## Group accessories

You can control multiple accessories with the tap of a button by grouping them.

1. Go to the Home app ![](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/06707ddf9262fd49e182d8fbea6c3f32.png) on your iPhone.

2. Tap the right side of an accessory tile, tap ![the Settings button](https://help.apple.com/assets/69F8EBBDF3B89A4F6E0C704C/69F8EBC43862495245036393/en_US/a3f742160163c000f2710f952646cafe.png), then tap Group with Other Accessories.

3. Tap the accessory you want to group with this accessory—another light in the room, for example.

4. In the Group Name field, type a name for the group.

5. Tap Done.

Turn on Include in Favorites to include the group in Favorites on the Home tab.
