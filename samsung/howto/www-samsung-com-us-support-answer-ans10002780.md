## Manage your backups
Once you delete a backup, there is no way to recover it. Make sure you have a separate backup or have copied the necessary files before proceeding with the deletion.
It's essential to regularly manage your backups to avoid issues with low storage space, which includes deleting old or unnecessary backups to free up space. Follow the steps below to delete backups you've created or content you've moved.
Samsung Cloud offers an option to delete backups directly from the Settings menu of your phone.
