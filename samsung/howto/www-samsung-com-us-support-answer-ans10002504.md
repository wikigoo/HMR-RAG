# Find your Samsung phone or tablet's IMEI, model, or serial number
The IMEI (or MEID) and serial number are two forms of identification for your Galaxy or . Just like fingerprints for people, these two numbers are different for every phone and tablet. These identifiers help ensure that your device isn't counterfeit and can also help you find it if it's lost or stolen. Additionally, sometimes you may need the IMEI or serial number to redeem special promotions from Samsung.
Available screens and settings may vary by wireless service provider, software version, and phone model.
